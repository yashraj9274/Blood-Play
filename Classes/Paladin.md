_"Holy knights sworn to an oath, wielding martial prowess and divine smites."_
**Mana Dependency** : 2/5
## Class Focus (Single-Class Only)
_Granted at Character Creation. Lost if you multiclass. Choose ONE:_
1. **Aegis of the Just:** Allies within 15ft share your Resistance modifier. If they take damage, you can choose to take it instead.
2. **Smite Evil:** Your Divine Smites deal double damage against Undead, Fiends, Aberrations, and Dragons.
3. **Unbreakable Vow:** As long as you adhere strictly to your oath, you are completely immune to being charmed, frightened, or mind-controlled.

### **Class Perk (Level 1): Divine Sense & Smite**
- **Divine Smite:** When you hit with a melee weapon attack, you can expend MP to deal radiant damage (1d8 per 10 MP spent, max 5d8).
- You can sense the presence of strong evil, good, undead, or fiends within 60ft.
- You gain proficiency with all armor, shields, and weapons.

### **Bonus Perk (Level 10 - Single-Class Only): Aura of the Crusader**
- Your aura increases to 30ft.
- You and friendly creatures within your aura cannot be frightened, and you all gain resistance to magic damage.

--- 

### **Level 1 Abilities (Choose 1):**
1. **Lay on Hands:** Touch a creature and heal them using a pool of HP equal to `Class Level x 10`. You can also cure diseases with this pool. (Cost: 1 AP)
2. **Command:** Speak a one-word command (Halt, Drop, Flee). Target must obey on its next turn (Will save negates). (Cost: 1 AP, 10 MP)
3. **Thunderous Smite:** Your next melee hit deals an extra 2d6 thunder damage and knocks the target prone (STR save). (Cost: 0 AP, Bonus, 10 MP)

### **Level 3 Abilities (Choose 1):**
1. **Compelled Duel:** Force an enemy to focus only on you. They have disadvantage attacking anyone else and cannot move away from you. (Cost: 1 AP, 10 MP)
2. **Wrathful Smite:** Your next melee hit deals an extra 1d6 psychic damage and frightens the target. (Cost: 0 AP, Bonus, 10 MP)
3. **Zone of Truth:** Create a 15ft sphere where no deliberate lies can be spoken (Will save). (Cost: 1 AP, 15 MP)

### **Level 5 Abilities (Choose 1):**
1. **Extra Attack:** Make two attacks when you take the Attack action. (Cost: 1 AP)
2. **Find Steed:** Summon a magical, highly intelligent warhorse (or other mount) that fights alongside you. (Cost: 1 AP, 20 MP)
3. **Blinding Smite:** Your next melee hit deals an extra 3d8 radiant damage and blinds the target for 1 minute. (Cost: 0 AP, Bonus, 20 MP)

### **Level 7 Abilities (Choose 1):**
1. **Aura of Devotion:** You and allies within 10ft can't be charmed. (Passive Aura)
2. **Aura of Warding:** You and allies within 10ft have resistance to damage from spells. (Passive Aura)
3. **Relentless Avenger:** When you hit with an opportunity attack, you can immediately move up to half your speed. (Passive)

### **Level 9 Abilities (Choose 1):**
1. **Revivify:** Bring an ally who died within the last minute back to life with 1 HP. (Cost: 1 AP, 50 MP)
2. **Crusader's Mantle:** Holy power radiates from you. Allies within 30ft deal an extra 1d4 radiant damage on weapon attacks. (Cost: 1 AP, 25 MP)
3. **Staggering Smite:** Your next melee hit deals an extra 4d6 psychic damage and gives the target disadvantage on all rolls next turn. (Cost: 0 AP, Bonus, 30 MP)

### **Level 11 Abilities (Choose 1):**
1. **Improved Divine Smite:** ALL your melee weapon attacks permanently deal an extra 1d8 radiant damage, for free. (Passive)
2. **Banishment:** Send a demon, devil, or elemental back to its home plane instantly (Will save). (Cost: 1 AP, 40 MP)
3. **Aura of Courage:** You and allies within 10ft can't be frightened. (Passive Aura)

### **Level 13 Abilities (Choose 1):**
1. **Find Greater Steed:** Summon a Griffon, Pegasus, or Dire Wolf as your mount. (Cost: 1 AP, 40 MP)
2. **Staggering Blow:** Your critical hits automatically stun the target for 1 turn. (Passive)
3. **Death Ward:** Protect a target. The next time they would drop to 0 HP, they drop to 1 HP instead. (Cost: 1 AP, 30 MP)

### **Level 15 Abilities (Choose 1):**
1. **Cleansing Touch:** You can use your action to end one spell or magical effect on yourself or a willing creature. (Cost: 1 AP)
2. **Banishing Smite:** Your next hit deals 5d10 force damage. If this drops the target below 50 HP, they are banished to another plane. (Cost: 0 AP, Bonus, 50 MP)
3. **Holy Nimbus:** Emit sunlight in a 30ft radius. Undead and fiends taking damage in this light take maximum possible damage. (Cost: 1 AP)

### **Level 17 Abilities (Choose 1):**
1. **Destructive Wave:** Strike the ground, creating a massive shockwave dealing 5d6 thunder and 5d6 radiant damage in a 30ft radius, knocking enemies prone. (Cost: 1 AP, 60 MP)
2. **Aura of Holy Fire:** Any enemy that starts its turn within 10ft of you takes 2d10 radiant damage automatically. (Passive Aura)
3. **Circle of Power:** Allies within 30ft have advantage on saves against magic, and take zero damage if they succeed. (Cost: 1 AP, 60 MP)

### **Level 19 Abilities (Choose 1):**
1. **Holy Champion:** Transform into an Avatar of Battle. Gain 100 AHP, advantage on all attacks, and your smites cost 0 MP for 1 minute. (Cost: 1 AP)
2. **True Resurrection:** Bring anyone back to life from any state, fully healed. (Cost: 2 AP, 150 MP)
3. **Invincible Legion:** You and all allies within 30ft become completely immune to physical damage for 3 turns. (Cost: 1 AP, 100 MP)

---

### **Level 2 Skills (Choose 1):**
1. **Fighting Style (Defense):** Gain +1 AC while wearing armor. (Passive)
2. **Fighting Style (Dueling):** +2 damage when wielding one one-handed weapon. (Passive)
3. **Fighting Style (Great Weapon):** Reroll 1s and 2s on damage dice with two-handed weapons. (Passive)

### **Level 4 Skills (Choose 1):**
1. **Channel Divinity: Turn the Unholy:** Fiends and Undead within 30ft must make a Will save or run away in terror. (Once/day)
2. **Channel Divinity: Vow of Enmity:** Choose one enemy. You gain advantage on all attack rolls against them for 1 minute. (Once/day)
3. **Divine Health:** You are completely immune to all diseases, magical or mundane. (Passive)

### **Level 6 Skills (Choose 1):**
1. **Aura of Protection:** You and friendly creatures within 10ft gain a bonus to all saving throws equal to your Charisma/Ingenuity modifier. (Passive)
2. **Righteous Vigor:** When you reduce an enemy to 0 HP, you regain HP equal to your Paladin level. (Passive)
3. **Shield Master:** If you are attacked, you can use your reaction to add your shield's AC to your saving throw or AC. (Passive)

### **Level 8 Skills (Choose 1):**
1. **Inspiring Smite:** When you use Divine Smite, you can distribute Temporary AHP equal to the damage dealt among allies within 30ft. (Once/day)
2. **Unyielding:** You cannot be knocked prone while conscious. (Passive)
3. **Holy Vision:** You can see perfectly in magical and mundane darkness up to 120ft. (Passive)

### **Level 10 Skills (Choose 1):**
1. **Aura of Resolve:** You and allies within 10ft can no longer be charmed or frightened. (Passive)
2. **Divine Fortitude:** Your Max HP increases by +Class Level x 5. (Passive)
3. **Blessed Warrior:** You learn two Cleric cantrips (like Sacred Flame or Spare the Dying) and can cast them for 0 MP. (Passive)

### **Level 12 Skills (Choose 1):**
1. **Unyielding Saint:** If an attack drops you to 0 HP, you can choose to drop to 1 HP instead. (Once/day)
2. **Purity of Spirit:** You are immune to curses and hexes. (Passive)
3. **Archangel's Step:** You can sprout ethereal wings and fly up to 60ft as a bonus action. (Once/day)

### **Level 14 Skills (Choose 1):**
1. **Cleansing Aura:** Allies who end their turn within 10ft of you are cured of one negative condition. (Passive)
2. **Vengeful Strike:** If an ally within 30ft is hit, you can instantly teleport to the attacker and make a melee attack against them. (Reaction, Once/day)
3. **Armor of Faith:** Your armor cannot be sundered, rusted, or destroyed by any means. (Passive)

### **Level 16 Skills (Choose 1):**
1. **Aura Expansion:** All your auras expand to a 30ft radius. (Passive)
2. **Divine Retribution:** When you are hit by a melee attack, the attacker takes radiant damage equal to your Charisma/Ingenuity modifier. (Passive)
3. **Incorruptible:** Your soul cannot be trapped, drained, or manipulated by magic. (Passive)

### **Level 18 Skills (Choose 1):**
1. **Holy Avenger:** Any weapon you hold acts as a legendary Holy Avenger sword, emitting an antimagic field and dealing +2d10 radiant damage to fiends/undead. (Passive)
2. **Martyr's Resurrection:** If you die, you release a massive explosion of healing energy, restoring all allies in 100ft to full HP and MP. (Passive)
3. **Perfect Smite:** Your next Divine Smite deals maximum possible damage automatically. (Once/day)

### **Level 20 Skills (Choose 1):**
1. **Exalted Champion:** For 1 hour, you are immune to all non-magical damage and your weapon attacks automatically hit. (Once/week)
2. **God's Right Hand:** You summon the physical weapon of your deity (e.g., a massive hammer of light) which strikes anywhere on the battlefield for 20d12 damage. (Once/day)
3. **Ascendant Vow:** Your oaths bind reality. You can forbid a specific action (e.g., "No spellcasting") in a 100ft radius. Anyone attempting it takes 10d10 radiant damage. (Once/day)
