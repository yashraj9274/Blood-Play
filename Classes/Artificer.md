_"Inventors, engineers, and masters of magical machinery."_
**Mana Dependency** : 1/5
## Class Focus (Single-Class Only)
_Granted at Character Creation. Lost if you multiclass. Choose ONE:_
1. **Overclock:** Your magical items, guns, and gadgets have a 50% chance not to consume durability or charges when used.
2. **Master Tinkerer:** You can attune to 2 additional magic items compared to normal mortals, and you gain +5 to all Ingenuity checks.
3. **Turret Master:** You can construct automated turrets that fire automatically at the end of your turn without costing AP.

### **Class Perk (Level 1): Magical Tinkering**
- You can infuse mundane objects with minor magical properties (light, sound, floating).
- Gain proficiency with all firearms, crossbows, and medium armor.
- You can use Intelligence instead of Strength for weapon attacks if the weapon is modified by you.

### **Bonus Perk (Level 10 - Single-Class Only): Soul of the Machine**
- You can transfer your consciousness into one of your mechanical constructs to avoid death.
- Constructs you build have double HP.

### **Level 1 Abilities (Choose 1):**
1. **Deploy Turret:** Place a stationary turret that shoots enemies for 1d8 piercing damage. (Cost: 1 AP)
2. **Arcane Weapon:** Infuse your weapon with magic, making it deal +1d6 elemental damage for 1 hour. (Cost: 1 AP)
3. **Shocking Grasp:** Melee attack dealing 1d8 lightning damage. Target cannot take reactions. (Cost: 1 AP)

### **Level 3 Abilities (Choose 1):**
1. **Forcefield Generator:** Create a 10ft bubble that grants 50 Temporary AHP to anyone inside. (Cost: 1 AP)
2. **Grappling Hook:** Pull yourself to a ledge or pull an enemy towards you. (Cost: 1 AP)
3. **Overload:** Force your turret to explode, dealing 3d8 fire damage to nearby enemies. (Cost: 1 AP)

### **Level 5 Abilities (Choose 1):**
1. **Extra Attack:** Make two attacks when you take the Attack action. (Cost: 1 AP)
2. **Mechanical Hound:** Summon a robotic dog that bites for 2d6 damage and can track enemies. (Cost: 1 AP)
3. **Jetpack Dash:** Fly up to 30ft in any direction without provoking opportunity attacks. (Cost: 1 AP)

### **Level 7 Abilities (Choose 1):**
1. **Flash-Bang Grenade:** Enemies in a 15ft radius must make a RES save or be blinded and deafened. (Cost: 1 AP)
2. **Healing Drone:** Deploy a drone that hovers near an ally, healing them for 1d8 HP at the start of their turn. (Cost: 1 AP)
3. **Magnetic Repulsion:** Push all metal-wearing enemies 20ft away from you. (Cost: 1 AP)

### **Level 9 Abilities (Choose 1):**
1. **Construct Mech Suit:** Climb into a robotic suit for 10 turns. Gain +50 Max HP and deal +2d8 physical damage. (Cost: 2 AP)
2. **Homing Missiles:** Fire three missiles that deal 2d10 fire damage each. They automatically hit. (Cost: 1 AP)
3. **EMP Blast:** Disable all enemy magical items, shields, and constructs in a 30ft radius for 2 turns. (Cost: 1 AP)

### **Level 11 Abilities (Choose 1):**
1. **Clockwork Swarm:** Release a swarm of tiny robots that deal 4d6 piercing damage and distract enemies (granting allies advantage). (Cost: 1 AP)
2. **Teleporter Pad:** Place two connected pads. Anyone stepping on one instantly teleports to the other. (Cost: 1 AP)
3. **Energy Shield:** Target ally gains immunity to energy-based damage (Fire, Lightning, Magic) for 1 round. (Cost: 1 AP)

### **Level 13 Abilities (Choose 1):**
1. **Orbital Strike:** Call down a massive beam from the sky dealing 10d10 radiant damage in a 20ft radius. (Cost: 2 AP)
2. **Time Dilation Field:** Enemies in a 30ft area move at half speed and lose 1 AP per turn. (Cost: 1 AP)
3. **Perfect Repair:** Fully restore the HP and ARN of any construct, vehicle, or armor. (Cost: 1 AP)

### **Level 15 Abilities (Choose 1):**
1. **Iron Golem:** Summon a massive golem that absorbs damage meant for you and hits for 4d12. (Cost: 1 AP)
2. **Disintegration Ray:** Deal 12d8 force damage. If this reduces a target to 0 HP, they are turned to dust. (Cost: 1 AP)
3. **Holographic Decoys:** Create 3 perfect illusions of yourself that confuse enemies and dodge attacks. (Cost: 1 AP)

### **Level 17 Abilities (Choose 1):**
1. **Gravity Manipulator:** Lift up to 5 enemies into the air, rendering them helpless, then slam them down for 8d10 damage. (Cost: 1 AP)
2. **Invulnerability Matrix:** You and all allies within 15ft become completely immune to all damage for 1 round. (Cost: 1 AP)
3. **Nanite Cloud:** Release a cloud that heals allies for 4d10 per turn and damages enemies for 4d10 per turn. (Cost: 1 AP)

### **Level 19 Abilities (Choose 1):**
1. **The Doomsday Device:** A catastrophic bomb dealing 30d12 damage in a 100ft radius. Hits everything. (Cost: 3 AP)
2. **God-Machine Link:** Your consciousness merges with your tech. You can control any machine worldwide and cast spells through them. (Cost: 1 AP)
3. **Quantum Eraser:** Completely erase a single target from reality (EXTREME INT save negates). (Cost: 2 AP)
