_"Masters of stealth, assassination, and exploiting enemy weaknesses."_
**Mana Dependency** : 2/5
## Class Focus (Single-Class Only)
_Granted at Character Creation. Lost if you multiclass. Choose ONE:_
1. **Shadow Strike:** Your attacks from stealth automatically bypass AHP and deal direct damage to the target's Core Health (CHP).
2. **Thief of Time:** You can take two bonus actions per turn instead of one.
3. **Ghost Step:** You can pass through solid walls, doors, and enemies as long as you end your turn in an open space.

### **Class Perk (Level 1): Sneak Attack & Expertise**
- **Sneak Attack:** Once per turn, you can deal an extra 1d6 damage to one creature you hit with an attack if you have advantage on the attack roll, or if another enemy of the target is within 5ft of it. This increases by 1d6 every odd level (2d6 at 3, 3d6 at 5, etc).
- **Expertise:** Choose two skills (e.g., Stealth, Deception). Your proficiency bonus is doubled for any ability check you make that uses them.

### **Bonus Perk (Level 10 - Single-Class Only): Master Assassin**
- Any hit you score against a creature that is surprised is automatically a critical hit.
- You have advantage on attack rolls against any creature that hasn't taken a turn in combat yet.

### **Level 1 Abilities (Choose 1):**
1. **Cunning Action:** You can take the Dash, Disengage, or Hide action as a bonus action. (Cost: 0 AP, Bonus)
2. **Assassinate:** Your first attack in combat deals double damage if you strike from hiding. (Passive)
3. **Fast Hands:** You can use a bonus action to pick a lock, disarm a trap, or use an item (like throwing a vial). (Cost: 0 AP, Bonus)

### **Level 3 Abilities (Choose 1):**
1. **Shadow Arts:** You can cast *Darkness*, *Darkvision*, *Pass without Trace*, or *Silence* using 15 MP. (Cost: 1 AP, 15 MP)
2. **Swashbuckler's Dance:** You can move through occupied enemy spaces, and your movement does not provoke opportunity attacks. (Passive)
3. **Poison Master:** Apply poison to your weapon as a free action. Next hit deals an extra 2d6 poison damage. (Cost: 0 AP)

### **Level 5 Abilities (Choose 1):**
1. **Uncanny Dodge:** When an attacker that you can see hits you with an attack, you can use your reaction to halve the attack's damage against you. (Reaction, Passive)
2. **Skirmisher:** You can move up to half your speed as a reaction when an enemy ends its turn within 5ft of you. (Reaction, Passive)
3. **Infiltration Expertise:** You can flawlessly mimic the speech, handwriting, and mannerisms of a person, and create a perfect false identity. (Passive)

### **Level 7 Abilities (Choose 1):**
1. **Evasion:** When subjected to an AoE effect that allows a DEX save for half damage, you take no damage on a success and half on a fail. (Passive)
2. **Panache:** Make a Charisma (Seduction/Deception) check contested by an enemy's Will. If you win, they are charmed by you or provoked into only attacking you. (Cost: 1 AP)
3. **Shadow Step:** You can teleport up to 60ft from one shadow to another shadow as a bonus action. (Cost: 0 AP, Bonus)

### **Level 9 Abilities (Choose 1):**
1. **Supreme Sneak:** You have advantage on a Stealth check if you move no more than half your speed on the same turn. (Passive)
2. **Death Strike:** When you hit a surprised creature, it must make a RES save. On a fail, the damage is doubled. (Passive)
3. **Use Magic Device:** You ignore all class, race, and level requirements on the use of magic items. (Passive)

### **Level 11 Abilities (Choose 1):**
1. **Reliable Talent:** Whenever you make an ability check that lets you add your proficiency bonus, you can treat a d20 roll of 9 or lower as a 10. (Passive)
2. **Cloak of Shadows:** Become completely invisible as long as you remain in dim light or darkness. (Cost: 1 AP)
3. **Elegant Maneuver:** You can use a bonus action to gain advantage on the next Agility or Acrobatics check you make on your turn. (Cost: 0 AP, Bonus)

### **Level 13 Abilities (Choose 1):**
1. **Impostor:** You can unerringly mimic another person's speech, writing, and behavior. You can forge documents perfectly. (Passive)
2. **Blindsense:** If you are able to hear, you are aware of the location of any hidden or invisible creature within 10ft of you. (Passive)
3. **Thief's Reflexes:** You can take two turns during the first round of any combat. (Passive)

### **Level 15 Abilities (Choose 1):**
1. **Slippery Mind:** You gain proficiency in Will saving throws. (Passive)
2. **Master Duelist:** If you miss with an attack roll, you can roll it again with advantage. You can do this once per short rest. (Cost: 0 AP)
3. **Shadowy Dodge:** You can use your reaction to halve the damage of any incoming attack you can see. (Reaction, Passive)

### **Level 17 Abilities (Choose 1):**
1. **Death Attack:** If you study a target for 3 rounds out of combat, your next attack against them instantly drops them to 0 HP if they fail a RES save. (Cost: 1 AP)
2. **Spell Thief:** Immediately after a creature casts a spell that targets you, you can force them to make a save. On a fail, you negate the spell and steal it, allowing you to cast it yourself. (Reaction, Cost: 0 AP)
3. **Strike of the Wind:** You can strike every enemy within a 30ft radius simultaneously in a blur of motion. (Cost: 1 AP)

### **Level 19 Abilities (Choose 1):**
1. **Stroke of Luck:** If your attack misses a target within range, you can turn the miss into a hit. Alternatively, if you fail an ability check, you can treat the d20 roll as a 20. (Once/short rest)
2. **Shadow Realm:** You can pull a target into a demiplane of shadows with you. You have advantage on all attacks there, and the target is blinded. (Cost: 1 AP)
3. **Assassination Contract:** Declare a target. Until that target dies, you deal double Sneak Attack damage against them. (Cost: 0 AP)

### **Level 2 Skills (Choose 1):**
1. **Second-Story Work:** Climbing no longer costs you extra movement, and you can make running jumps with only a 5ft running start. (Passive)
2. **Streetwise:** You can find the black market, assassins guild, or criminal underbelly of any city in 1 hour. (Passive)
3. **Quick Fingers:** You can pickpocket or plant an item on someone in combat as a bonus action. (Passive)

### **Level 4 Skills (Choose 1):**
1. **Skulker:** You can hide when you are only lightly obscured, and missing with a ranged attack from hiding doesn't reveal you. (Passive)
2. **Expert Forgery:** You can craft fake documents, IDs, and royal seals that pass absolute scrutiny. (Passive)
3. **Silent Killer:** If you reduce a target to 0 HP, they make no noise when they die or fall. (Passive)

### **Level 6 Skills (Choose 1):**
1. **Expertise II:** Choose two more skills to gain double proficiency in. (Passive)
2. **Read Lips:** You can perfectly read lips of anyone speaking a language you understand, up to 100ft away. (Passive)
3. **Escape Artist:** You have advantage on all checks to escape grapples, handcuffs, ropes, or magical restraints. (Passive)

### **Level 8 Skills (Choose 1):**
1. **Evasion Master:** You take no damage from falling up to 100ft. (Passive)
2. **Venomous Coating:** Your weapons permanently deal an extra 1d4 poison damage. (Passive)
3. **Acrobatic Dodge:** You can use your Agility modifier instead of your Resistance modifier for your ARN calculation. (Passive)

### **Level 10 Skills (Choose 1):**
1. **Supreme Sneak:** You have advantage on all Stealth checks. Period. (Passive)
2. **Master of Disguise:** You can create a flawless physical disguise for yourself or an ally in 1 minute. (Once/day)
3. **Cunning Stalker:** You can move at full speed while completely silent and hidden. (Passive)

### **Level 12 Skills (Choose 1):**
1. **Iron Will:** You have advantage on saving throws against being charmed or frightened. (Passive)
2. **Unerring Eye:** You can automatically detect optical illusions, holograms, and shapechangers. (Passive)
3. **Ghost Step:** You can walk through solid walls up to 5ft thick. (Once/day)

### **Level 14 Skills (Choose 1):**
1. **Blindsense:** You can fight effectively while blinded, perceiving everything in a 30ft radius. (Passive)
2. **Vanish:** You can turn invisible for 1 minute. (Once/day)
3. **Saboteur:** You know exactly where to strike an object (door, lock, vault, bridge) to destroy it instantly. (Passive)

### **Level 16 Skills (Choose 1):**
1. **Elusive:** No attack roll has advantage against you while you aren't incapacitated. (Passive)
2. **Crippling Strike:** When you deal Sneak Attack damage, you permanently reduce the target's Strength by 2. (Passive)
3. **Shadow Cloak:** You are completely immune to scrying, magical tracking, and mind reading. (Passive)

### **Level 18 Skills (Choose 1):**
1. **Uncanny Senses:** You cannot be surprised, ever. You always act first in initiative. (Passive)
2. **Master Assassin:** If you attack a surprised target, they must make a RES save or die instantly. (Passive)
3. **Time Thief:** You can stop time for 1 round. (Once/day)

### **Level 20 Skills (Choose 1):**
1. **Avatar of Shadows:** You become one with the darkness. You are invisible permanently (you can suppress this at will), and you can teleport anywhere in the world that is in shadow. (Passive)
2. **The Perfect Crime:** You can steal a concept (like a memory, a talent, or a name) from a target. (Once/week)
3. **Absolute Evasion:** You can perfectly dodge any one attack, spell, or catastrophic event (like a nuke), taking 0 damage. (Once/day)
