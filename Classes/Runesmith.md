_"Artisans of ancient magic, carving raw elemental power into stone and steel."_
**Mana Dependency** : 3/5
## Class Focus (Single-Class Only)
_Granted at Character Creation. Lost if you multiclass. Choose ONE:_
1. **Runic Master:** You can carve runes instantaneously (costs 0 AP) and you can maintain double the normal amount of active runes simultaneously.
2. **Explosive Glyphs:** Any defensive or utility rune you carve also acts as a trap. If an enemy steps on it or attacks the warded object, it explodes for 3d6 force damage.
3. **Runic Skin:** Runes permanently etched into your skin grant you a permanent +10 to ARN and resistance to all elemental damage.

### **Class Perk (Level 1): Rune Carving**
- You use Intelligence as your spellcasting and rune-carving modifier.
- You can carve Runes onto weapons, armor, or the ground. A carved Rune lasts until triggered or until you take a long rest.
- You gain proficiency with heavy armor, shields, and warhammers.

### **Bonus Perk (Level 10 - Single-Class Only): Master Forge**
- You can forge magical weapons and armor during a long rest using raw materials.
- Any weapon you wield that has a rune carved into it deals double its base damage dice.

### **Level 1 Abilities (Choose 1):**
1. **Rune of Fire:** Etch a rune on a weapon. Its next hit deals an extra 1d10 fire damage and ignites the target. (Cost: 1 AP, 5 MP)
2. **Rune of Shielding:** Etch a rune on an ally's armor. It absorbs the next 20 points of damage they take. (Cost: 1 AP, 10 MP)
3. **Earth Tremor Rune:** Place a rune on the floor. When an enemy steps within 5ft, it explodes, dealing 1d8 bludgeoning damage and knocking them prone. (Cost: 1 AP, 5 MP)

### **Level 3 Abilities (Choose 1):**
1. **Rune of Speed:** Etch a rune on boots. The wearer's movement speed is doubled for 1 minute. (Cost: 1 AP, 10 MP)
2. **Lightning Glyph:** Place a trap rune. Triggers a 20ft line of lightning dealing 3d8 lightning damage. (Cost: 1 AP, 15 MP)
3. **Sunder Armor:** Strike an enemy with your hammer, transferring a corrosive rune that permanently lowers their ARN by 2. (Cost: 1 AP, 10 MP)

### **Level 5 Abilities (Choose 1):**
1. **Extra Attack:** Make two attacks when you take the Attack action. (Cost: 1 AP)
2. **Rune of Binding:** Etch a rune onto a target you hit. They are paralyzed by magical chains for 1 turn (STR save negates). (Cost: 0 AP, Bonus, 15 MP)
3. **Rune of Healing:** Touch an ally and carve a glowing mark. They heal 2d8 + INT. (Cost: 1 AP, 15 MP)

### **Level 7 Abilities (Choose 1):**
1. **Rune of Silence:** Create a 20ft zone where no sound can exist, preventing spellcasting. (Cost: 1 AP, 15 MP)
2. **Ward of Rebound:** A warded ally reflects 50% of any melee damage they take back at the attacker for 1 round. (Cost: 1 AP, 20 MP)
3. **Rune of Flight:** Carve a rune on an object or person, granting them a 60ft fly speed for 10 minutes. (Cost: 1 AP, 20 MP)

### **Level 9 Abilities (Choose 1):**
1. **Rune of Annihilation:** The next time this warded weapon hits, it deals an extra 6d10 force damage and destroys the target's armor completely. (Cost: 1 AP, 40 MP)
2. **Glyph of Warding:** Scribe a hidden magical trap that can store any spell you know, triggering when specific conditions are met. (Cost: 1 AP, 30 MP)
3. **Rune of Earthquake:** Slam your hammer down. A 40ft radius becomes difficult terrain, and all enemies take 4d6 bludgeoning damage. (Cost: 1 AP, 30 MP)

### **Level 11 Abilities (Choose 1):**
1. **Rune of Nullification:** Target's magical buffs, resistances, and immunities are completely stripped away for 1 minute. (Cost: 1 AP, 40 MP)
2. **Golem Smithing:** Carve a control rune into a pile of rocks or metal, animating an Earth Golem to fight for you. (Cost: 2 AP, 50 MP)
3. **Rune of Teleportation:** Create two linked runes. Anyone stepping on one instantly teleports to the other. (Cost: 1 AP, 30 MP)

### **Level 13 Abilities (Choose 1):**
1. **Meteor Rune:** Paint a massive rune on the ground. Next turn, a meteor strikes that exact spot for 10d10 fire damage. (Cost: 1 AP, 60 MP)
2. **Rune of True Sight:** Grant an ally the ability to see perfectly in magical darkness, see invisible creatures, and see through illusions. (Cost: 1 AP, 25 MP)
3. **Adamantine Carving:** Your weapon becomes completely unbreakable and ignores all physical resistances automatically. (Passive)

### **Level 15 Abilities (Choose 1):**
1. **Rune of Invulnerability:** For 1 round, the warded target takes absolutely 0 damage from any source. (Cost: 1 AP, 60 MP)
2. **Word of Recall Rune:** Teleport yourself and up to 5 allies back to a permanent safehouse rune you previously carved. (Cost: 1 AP, 50 MP)
3. **Rune of Domination:** Carve a rune onto a creature's forehead (melee attack). They are permanently mind-controlled by you until the rune is destroyed. (Cost: 1 AP, 80 MP)

### **Level 17 Abilities (Choose 1):**
1. **Rune of the Gods:** A weapon etched with this rune deals maximum damage on every single damage die rolled. Lasts 1 minute. (Cost: 1 AP, 100 MP)
2. **Cataclysm Glyph:** A trap rune that, when triggered, creates an explosion dealing 20d8 force damage in a 100ft radius. (Cost: 1 AP, 100 MP)
3. **Rune of Resurrection:** If a creature wearing this rune dies, they instantly revive at full HP. The rune shatters afterward. (Cost: Ritual, 150 MP)

### **Level 19 Abilities (Choose 1):**
1. **Master of the Forge:** You can cast *Wish* once per week by spending 8 hours carving a perfectly flawless rune of reality-bending power. (Cost: 0 AP)
2. **Avatar of Stone:** You turn into a living mountain for 1 minute. You gain 500 AHP, deal double physical damage, and cannot be moved. (Cost: 1 AP, 80 MP)
3. **World-Rending Strike:** Strike the ground to create a massive chasm 1 mile long and 100 feet deep, swallowing armies whole. (Cost: 2 AP, 150 MP)

### **Level 2 Skills (Choose 1):**
1. **Dwarven Resilience:** You have advantage on saving throws against poison, and you have resistance against poison damage. (Passive)
2. **Appraisal:** You instantly know the exact value, magical properties, and history of any crafted item you hold. (Passive)
3. **Steady Posture:** You cannot be knocked prone by any attack that deals less than 20 damage. (Passive)

### **Level 4 Skills (Choose 1):**
1. **Quick Etch:** You can carve one basic rune (Level 1) as a free action once per combat. (Once/day)
2. **Runic Sight:** You can instantly detect any magical traps, invisible runes, or hidden doorways within 30ft. (Passive)
3. **Iron Stomach:** You can safely eat rocks, gems, or spoiled food without getting sick. (Passive)

### **Level 6 Skills (Choose 1):**
1. **Rune Extension:** The range of all your explosive runes is doubled. (Passive)
2. **Armor Reinforcement:** Any armor you wear grants an additional +2 to its base AC. (Passive)
3. **Magical Tinkering:** You can permanently enchant a weapon to glow in the dark or deal +1 magic damage. (Passive)

### **Level 8 Skills (Choose 1):**
1. **Elemental Affinity:** Choose an element. Your runes of that type cost half the MP to carve. (Passive)
2. **Unbreakable Focus:** You have advantage on all Intelligence and Will saving throws. (Passive)
3. **Heavy Hitter:** If you wield a weapon with two hands, you can push the target 10ft away on a hit. (Passive)

### **Level 10 Skills (Choose 1):**
1. **Master Engraver:** Your traps and glyphs are completely invisible to anyone without True Sight. (Passive)
2. **Forge Fire:** You are completely immune to fire and heat damage. (Passive)
3. **Stone Camouflage:** You have advantage on Stealth checks when hiding against stone or underground. (Passive)

### **Level 12 Skills (Choose 1):**
1. **Runic Echo:** When one of your trap runes detonates, it immediately reforms and can detonate one more time. (Passive)
2. **Soul of the Anvil:** You no longer need to sleep; you simply meditate while remaining fully aware of your surroundings. (Passive)
3. **Shatterstrike:** Critical hits with your melee weapon destroy the target's shield automatically. (Passive)

### **Level 14 Skills (Choose 1):**
1. **Aegis of the Maker:** You constantly project a 10ft aura. Allies inside take 10 less damage from every physical attack. (Passive Aura)
2. **Flawless Design:** You can never critically fail (roll a 1) on an attack roll or saving throw. (Passive)
3. **Runic Healing:** Whenever you carve a rune on an ally, they also heal for 1d10 HP. (Passive)

### **Level 16 Skills (Choose 1):**
1. **Titan's Grip:** You can wield massive two-handed weapons in one hand without penalty. (Passive)
2. **Rune of the Mind:** You become immune to all telepathy, mind-reading, and charm effects. (Passive)
3. **Earth Glide:** You can walk through solid rock and stone as easily as walking through air. (Passive)

### **Level 18 Skills (Choose 1):**
1. **Immortal Artisan:** You stop aging entirely and are immune to diseases and curses. (Passive)
2. **Perfect Symmetry:** Every time you roll damage for a rune, you automatically reroll any 1s or 2s. (Passive)
3. **Runic Ascendancy:** You can have three different Class Focuses simultaneously. (Passive)

### **Level 20 Skills (Choose 1):**
1. **The Prime Rune:** You know the true name of magic. You can instantly dispel any magical effect, barrier, or summoned creature simply by looking at it. (Once/day)
2. **God-Forged:** Your ARN permanently becomes 50, and your Max HP doubles. (Passive)
3. **Creator's Spark:** You can permanently turn any non-magical item into an Artifact-tier magic item. (Once/year)
