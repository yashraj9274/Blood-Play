_"Mages who derive their magic from a dangerous pact with a powerful patron."_
**Mana Dependency** : 5/5
## Class Focus (Single-Class Only)
_Granted at Character Creation. Lost if you multiclass. Choose ONE:_
1. **Pact's Price:** When you roll maximum damage on any spell die (e.g., an 8 on a d8), the die "explodes." You roll it again and add the new damage. This can chain infinitely.
2. **Eldritch Master:** Your Eldritch Blast automatically fires one additional beam, and its damage dice become d12s instead of d10s.
3. **Dark Bargain:** You can cast any spell you know without spending MP by paying HP equal to 5x the MP cost instead.

### **Class Perk (Level 1): Pact Magic**
- You use Charisma as your spellcasting modifier.
- You have a smaller MP pool than Wizards, but all your spells are automatically cast at their maximum possible level.
- You regain all your MP on a short rest.

### **Bonus Perk (Level 10 - Single-Class Only): Patron's Favor**
- Choose a Pact Boon (Blade, Tome, or Chain). You gain all the benefits of that boon perfectly optimized.
- You can beseech your patron once per week to instantly win any one battle, solve a puzzle, or resurrect a friend (consequences may apply).

### **Level 1 Abilities (Choose 1):**
1. **Eldritch Blast:** Fire a beam of crackling energy. Deals 1d10 + CHA force damage. Fires additional beams at higher levels. (Cost: 1 AP, 0 MP)
2. **Hex:** Curse a target. You deal an extra 1d6 necrotic damage to them whenever you hit them with an attack. (Cost: 0 AP, Bonus, 10 MP)
3. **Hellish Rebuke:** When damaged by an attacker you can see, you wreath them in hellfire dealing 2d10 fire damage (RES save half). (Cost: 1 AP, Reaction, 10 MP)

### **Level 3 Abilities (Choose 1):**
1. **Darkness:** Create a 15ft sphere of magical darkness that normal darkvision cannot pierce. (Cost: 1 AP, 15 MP)
2. **Misty Step:** Teleport up to 30ft to an unoccupied space you can see. (Cost: 0 AP, Bonus, 15 MP)
3. **Armor of Agathys:** Surround yourself with spectral frost. Gain 15 Temp HP. Anyone who hits you in melee takes 15 cold damage. (Cost: 1 AP, 15 MP)

### **Level 5 Abilities (Choose 1):**
1. **Hunger of Hadar:** Open a gateway to the dark void. A 20ft sphere fills with blackness, dealing 2d6 cold and 2d6 acid damage to anyone inside. (Cost: 1 AP, 25 MP)
2. **Counterspell:** Interrupt a creature in the process of casting a spell, completely negating it. (Cost: 1 AP, Reaction, 20 MP)
3. **Vampiric Touch:** Touch attack dealing 3d6 necrotic damage. You heal HP equal to the damage dealt. (Cost: 1 AP, 20 MP)

### **Level 7 Abilities (Choose 1):**
1. **Banishment:** Send an extraplanar creature back to its home plane, or send a native creature to a harmless demiplane for 1 minute (Will save). (Cost: 1 AP, 30 MP)
2. **Dimension Door:** Teleport yourself and one ally up to 500ft in any direction. (Cost: 1 AP, 35 MP)
3. **Shadow of Moil:** Flame-like shadows cover you. You become heavily obscured, gain resistance to radiant damage, and attackers take 2d8 necrotic damage. (Cost: 1 AP, 35 MP)

### **Level 9 Abilities (Choose 1):**
1. **Hold Monster:** Paralyze any creature (not just humanoids) for 1 minute (Will save negates). (Cost: 1 AP, 45 MP)
2. **Synaptic Static:** Deal 8d6 psychic damage in a 20ft radius. Targets failing a Will save subtract 1d6 from all attacks for 1 minute. (Cost: 1 AP, 40 MP)
3. **Contact Other Plane:** Mentally contact your Patron to ask 5 questions. Failure on an INT save drives you insane for 10 minutes. (Cost: Ritual, 30 MP)

### **Level 11 Abilities (Choose 1):**
1. **Mass Suggestion:** Magically influence up to 12 creatures to follow a completely reasonable-sounding course of action for 24 hours. (Cost: 1 AP, 50 MP)
2. **Circle of Death:** A sphere of negative energy ripples out in a 60ft radius, dealing 8d6 necrotic damage to everything. (Cost: 1 AP, 60 MP)
3. **True Seeing:** Grant yourself the ability to see all things as they actually are (invisible, illusions, shapechangers) for 1 hour. (Cost: 1 AP, 45 MP)

### **Level 13 Abilities (Choose 1):**
1. **Finger of Death:** Point at a creature. Deals 7d8 + 30 necrotic damage. If killed, they permanently become a zombie under your command. (Cost: 1 AP, 75 MP)
2. **Forcecage:** Trap a creature in an invisible, unbreakable box of force magic for 1 hour. (Cost: 1 AP, 70 MP)
3. **Crown of Stars:** Create 7 star-motes. You can launch one as a bonus action each turn for 4d12 radiant damage. (Cost: 1 AP, 65 MP)

### **Level 15 Abilities (Choose 1):**
1. **Feeblemind:** Shatter a target's intellect. They can no longer cast spells or communicate (Will save negates). (Cost: 1 AP, 80 MP)
2. **Maddening Darkness:** Create a 60ft sphere of absolute magical darkness. Targets inside take 8d8 psychic damage per turn and may go insane. (Cost: 1 AP, 85 MP)
3. **Dominate Monster:** Take complete control of any creature's actions for 1 hour. (Cost: 1 AP, 80 MP)

### **Level 17 Abilities (Choose 1):**
1. **Foresight:** Touch a willing creature. They gain advantage on ALL rolls, and disadvantage on ALL attacks against them for 8 hours. (Cost: 1 AP, 100 MP)
2. **Power Word Kill:** Utter a single word of death. If the target has less than 100 HP, they die instantly. (Cost: 1 AP, 120 MP)
3. **Psychic Scream:** Scream into the minds of up to 10 enemies. Deals 14d6 psychic damage and stuns them. If killed, their heads explode. (Cost: 1 AP, 110 MP)

### **Level 19 Abilities (Choose 1):**
1. **True Polymorph:** Permanently turn yourself, an ally, or an enemy into any creature or object of equal or lower CR. (Cost: 2 AP, 150 MP)
2. **Imprisonment:** Bind a creature permanently in a state of suspended animation, buried deep beneath the earth or chained in another dimension. (Cost: 2 AP, 130 MP)
3. **Eldritch Nuke:** Combine all your Eldritch Blast beams into a single, world-shattering shot that deals 20d12 force damage in a 100ft line. (Cost: 2 AP, 100 MP)

### **Level 2 Skills (Choose 1):**
1. **Agonizing Blast:** Add your Charisma modifier to the damage of every Eldritch Blast beam. (Passive)
2. **Devil's Sight:** You can see normally in darkness, both magical and nonmagical, to a distance of 120ft. (Passive)
3. **Beguiling Influence:** You gain proficiency in the Deception and Seduction skills. (Passive)

### **Level 4 Skills (Choose 1):**
1. **Repelling Blast:** When you hit a creature with Eldritch Blast, you can push the creature 10ft away from you in a straight line. (Passive)
2. **Armor of Shadows:** You can cast *Mage Armor* on yourself at will, without expending MP. (Passive)
3. **Fiendish Vigor:** You can cast *False Life* on yourself at will, gaining infinite out-of-combat Temp HP. (Passive)

### **Level 6 Skills (Choose 1):**
1. **Dark One's Blessing:** When you reduce a hostile creature to 0 HP, you gain temporary HP equal to your CHA mod + Class Level. (Passive)
2. **Entropic Ward:** When a creature attacks you, use your reaction to impose disadvantage on that roll. If it misses, your next attack against them has advantage. (Passive)
3. **Awakened Mind:** You can communicate telepathically with any creature you can see within 30ft. (Passive)

### **Level 8 Skills (Choose 1):**
1. **Eldritch Spear:** The range of your Eldritch Blast is increased to 300ft. (Passive)
2. **One with Shadows:** When you are in an area of dim light or darkness, you can use your action to become completely invisible. (Passive)
3. **Tomb of Levistus:** As a reaction when taking damage, encase yourself in ice. You gain 100 Temp HP, taking the damage to the ice instead, but you are incapacitated until your next turn. (Once/day)

### **Level 10 Skills (Choose 1):**
1. **Thought Shield:** Your thoughts can't be read, and you have resistance to psychic damage. Whenever a creature deals psychic damage to you, that creature takes the same amount of damage. (Passive)
2. **Fiendish Resilience:** Choose one damage type (bludgeoning, piercing, slashing). You gain resistance to it. (Passive)
3. **Beguiling Defenses:** You are immune to being charmed. (Passive)

### **Level 12 Skills (Choose 1):**
1. **Lifedrinker:** When you hit a creature with your pact weapon, the creature takes extra necrotic damage equal to your Charisma modifier. (Passive)
2. **Eldritch Smite:** Once per turn when you hit a creature with your pact weapon, you can expend 20 MP to deal an extra 4d8 force damage and knock them prone. (Passive)
3. **Ghostly Gaze:** You can see through solid objects up to 30ft away. (Once/day for 1 minute)

### **Level 14 Skills (Choose 1):**
1. **Hurl Through Hell:** When you hit a creature with an attack, you can instantly transport them through the lower planes. They return at the end of your next turn and take 10d10 psychic damage. (Once/day)
2. **Create Thrall:** You can touch an incapacitated humanoid. That creature is permanently charmed by you and follows your telepathic commands. (Once/day)
3. **Dark Delirium:** Plunge a creature into an illusory realm. They are charmed or frightened by you and cannot see/hear anything else for 1 minute. (Once/day)

### **Level 16 Skills (Choose 1):**
1. **Master of Hexes:** You can move your *Hex* curse to a new target as a free action without expending MP. (Passive)
2. **Shroud of Shadow:** You can cast *Invisibility* at will, without expending MP. (Passive)
3. **Witch Sight:** You can see the true form of any shapechanger or creature concealed by illusion or transmutation magic within 30ft. (Passive)

### **Level 18 Skills (Choose 1):**
1. **Eldritch Mastery:** You can spend 1 minute entreating your patron to regain all your expended MP. (Once/day)
2. **Pact of Immortality:** You stop aging and cannot be killed by massive damage. (Passive)
3. **Aura of the Patron:** You emit a 30ft aura. All allies within it gain your Charisma modifier to their saving throws. (Passive)

### **Level 20 Skills (Choose 1):**
1. **Avatar of the Patron:** You channel your patron directly into your body. For 1 minute, you are immune to all damage, you have infinite MP, and all your spells deal maximum damage automatically. (Once/week)
2. **Contract Rewrite:** You can cast *Wish* without expending MP to alter the fabric of reality. (Once/month)
3. **Soul Harvester:** Whenever you kill a creature, its soul is trapped in your weapon. You can consume trapped souls to permanently increase your Max HP by 5 per soul. (Passive)
