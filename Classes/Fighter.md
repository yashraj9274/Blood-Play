_"Masters of combat and weaponry."_

**Mana Dependency** : 1/5
## Class Focus (Single-Class Only)
_Granted at Character Creation. Lost if you multiclass._
**Weapon Master - Critical hits deal x3 damage and ignore physical resistance.**
### **Class Perk (Level 1): Combat Training**

- Gain proficiency with all weapons and armor
    
- +2 to attack rolls with any weapon
    
- Can use STR or Agility for attack rolls (whichever is higher)
    

### **Bonus Perk (Level 10 - Single-Class Only): Weapon Master**

- **Critical Mastery:** Critical hits with this weapon deal ×3 damage
    
- **Weapon Bond:** This weapon gains +2 to attack and damage rolls for you
    
- Ignores resistance to physical damage


--- 

### **Level 1 Abilities (Choose 1):**

1. **Action Surge:** Gain an extra action this turn. (Cost: 1 AP)
    
2. **Second Wind:** Heal 1d10 + Class Level HP. (Cost: 1 AP)
    
3. **Shield Bash:** Make a melee attack with your shield (1d4 + STR). If it hits, target must make STR save or be knocked prone. (Cost: 1 AP)
    

### **Level 3 Abilities (Choose 1):**

1. **Parry:** When hit by a melee attack, reduce damage by 1d10 + Agility. (Cost: 1 AP, Reaction)
    
2. **Precision Strike:** Add 1d8 to your next attack roll. (Cost: 1 AP)
    
3. **Disarming Attack:** On a hit, force target to drop one held item (STR save). (Cost: 1 AP)
    

### **Level 5 Abilities (Choose 1):**

1. **Extra Attack:** Make two attacks when you take the Attack action. (Cost: 1 AP)
    
2. **Rallying Cry:** Grant yourself and one ally temporary HP equal to Class Level × 2. (Cost: 1 AP)
    
3. **Pushing Attack:** On a hit, push target 15 feet away (STR save negates). (Cost: 1 AP)
    

### **Level 7 Abilities (Choose 1):**

1. **Indomitable:** Reroll a failed saving throw. (Cost: 1 AP)
    
2. **Maneuvering Attack:** On a hit, an ally can move up to half their speed without provoking opportunity attacks. (Cost: 1 AP)
    
3. **Goading Attack:** On a hit, target has disadvantage attacking anyone but you until your next turn. (Cost: 1 AP)
    

### **Level 9 Abilities (Choose 1):**

1. **Improved Critical:** Critical hit range increases to 18-20.
    
2. **Brute Force:** Add +Class Level damage to your next successful attack. (Cost: 1 AP)
    
3. **Cleave:** If you reduce a creature to 0 HP, make an additional attack against another creature within reach. (Cost: 1 AP)
    

### **Level 11 Abilities (Choose 1):**

1. **Whirlwind Attack:** Attack all creatures within 5 feet. (Cost: 1 AP)
    
2. **Tactical Assessment:** Learn target's HP, weaknesses, and resistances. (Cost: 1 AP)
    
3. **Evasive Footwork:** Your movement doesn't provoke opportunity attacks this turn. (Cost: 1 AP)
    

### **Level 13 Abilities (Choose 1):**

1. **Weapon Mastery:** Choose a weapon type. Attacks with it deal +2d6 damage for 1 minute. (Cost: 1 AP)
    
2. **Armor Breaker:** Your next attack ignores 50% of target's ARN. (Cost: 1 AP)
    
3. **Defensive Stance:** Gain +2 AC and resistance to physical damage until start of your next turn. (Cost: 1 AP)
    

### **Level 15 Abilities (Choose 1):**

1. **Relentless:** When reduced to 0 HP, drop to 1 HP instead. Usable once per long rest. (Cost: 1 AP)
    
2. **Perfect Parry:** When hit by an attack, you can choose to take no damage. (Cost: 1 AP, Reaction)
    
3. **Sunder Armor:** On a hit, reduce target's armor AHP by an additional 50%. (Cost: 1 AP)
    

### **Level 17 Abilities (Choose 1):**

1. **Survivor:** At start of your turn, regain 5 + RES mod HP if below half HP. (Passive)
    
2. **Devastating Critical:** Critical hits deal ×3 damage instead of ×2. (Passive)
    
3. **Counterattack:** When missed by an attack, make an immediate attack against the attacker. (Cost: 1 AP, Reaction)
    

### **Level 19 Abilities (Choose 1):**

1. **Ultimate Warrior:** Take two additional actions this turn. Usable once per long rest. (Cost: 1 AP)
    
2. **Perfect Stance:** Gain resistance to all damage and immunity to conditions for 1 round. (Cost: 1 AP)
    
3. **Master's Strike:** Your next attack automatically hits and scores a critical hit. (Cost: 1 AP)

### **Level 2 Skills (Choose 1):**

1. **Fighting Style (Archery):** Gain +2 to Aim checks. Ranged attacks ignore half cover. (Once/day, lasts 1 hour)
    
2. **Fighting Style (Defense):** +1 AC while wearing armor. (Once/day, lasts 1 hour)
    
3. **Fighting Style (Dueling):** +2 damage when wielding one one-handed weapon. (Once/day, lasts 1 hour)

1. **Battle Tactics:** Grant all allies within 30ft advantage on their next attack. (Once/day)
    
2. **Intimidating Presence:** Force all enemies within 30ft to make Will save or be frightened for 1 minute. (Once/day)
    
3. **Tactical Retreat:** You and allies within 30ft can Disengage and move up to half speed. (Once/day)
    

### **Level 6 Skills (Choose 1):**

1. **Hold the Line:** Creatures provoke opportunity attacks when entering your reach. Lasts 1 minute. (Once/day)
    
2. **Weapon Bond:** Choose a weapon. You can't be disarmed of it, and it returns if thrown. (Once/day, lasts 24 hours)
    
3. **Shield Wall:** You and adjacent allies gain +2 AC for 1 minute. (Once/day)
    

### **Level 8 Skills (Choose 1):**

1. **Brace for Impact:** Gain resistance to all damage until start of your next turn. (Once/day)
    
2. **Precision Targeting:** Your next attack ignores cover and has advantage. (Once/day)
    
3. **Shield Slam:** Make a shield attack that deals 2d6 + STR damage and knocks target prone (STR save). (Once/day)
    

### **Level 10 Skills (Choose 1):**

1. **Weapon Specialization:** Choose one weapon type. Deal +Class Level damage with it for 1 hour. (Once/day)
    
2. **Unbreakable:** Gain immunity to being stunned, paralyzed, or knocked prone for 1 minute. (Once/day)
    
3. **Rally:** Heal all allies within 30ft for 2d6 + Class Level HP. (Once/day)
    

### **Level 12 Skills (Choose 1):**

1. **Critical Defense:** When an attack would critically hit you, it becomes a normal hit instead. (Once/day)
    
2. **Disarming Sweep:** Force all enemies within 10ft to make DEX save or drop their weapons. (Once/day)
    
3. **Tireless:** You don't suffer exhaustion and can act normally while at 0 HP for 1 minute. (Once/day)
    

### **Level 14 Skills (Choose 1):**

1. **Weapon Supremacy:** Your attacks automatically hit for 1 round (still roll damage). (Once/day)
    
2. **Indomitable Might:** Your STR score becomes 24 for 1 minute. (Once/day)
    
3. **Battlefield Control:** You can make opportunity attacks against all enemies who move within your reach for 1 round. (Once/day)
    

### **Level 16 Skills (Choose 1):**

1. **Armor Mastery:** Your ARN doubles for 1 minute. (Once/day)
    
2. **Perfect Dodge:** Automatically succeed on one DEX save. (Once/day)
    
3. **Sundering Blow:** Your next attack destroys the target's armor if it hits. (Once/day)
    

### **Level 18 Skills (Choose 1):**

1. **Legendary Resistance:** Automatically succeed on one saving throw. (Once/day)
    
2. **Whirlwind of Death:** Make a melee attack against every creature within 10 feet. (Once/day)
    
3. **Unstoppable:** You can't be grappled, restrained, or moved against your will for 1 minute. (Once/day)
    

### **Level 20 Skills (Choose 1):**

1. **Last Stand:** Gain 100 temporary HP that last until depleted. (Once/day)
    
2. **Perfect Warrior:** Gain all Fighting Styles simultaneously for 1 minute. (Once/day)
    
3. **Army of One:** You can take 3 actions on your turn instead of 1. (Once/day)
