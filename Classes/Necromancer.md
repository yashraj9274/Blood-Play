_"Masters of death magic, raising the fallen and draining the life from the living."_
**Mana Dependency** : 4/5
## Class Focus (Single-Class Only)
_Granted at Character Creation. Lost if you multiclass. Choose ONE:_
1. **Legion:** You have no cap on the number of undead minions you can control simultaneously, provided you have the MP to sustain them.
2. **Life Leech:** 50% of all necrotic damage you deal with spells or weapons is instantly returned to you as healing (HP).
3. **Death's Door:** You cannot die from massive damage. Any hit that would kill you instead reduces you to 1 HP, and the damage destroys one of your active minions instead.

### **Class Perk (Level 1): Dark Arts**
- You can cast Necromancy spells using MP. Intelligence is your spellcasting stat.
- You can command your undead minions as a free action on your turn.
- Minions you raise have base stats equivalent to standard Skeletons or Zombies, but they gain bonus HP equal to your Class Level x 5.

### **Bonus Perk (Level 10 - Single-Class Only): Lord of the Grave**
- Any undead you raise are permanent until destroyed (they don't decay or turn to dust after a set time).
- You can cast *Animate Dead* without expending MP once per day.

### **Level 1 Abilities (Choose 1):**
1. **Chill Touch:** A spectral hand grips the target. Deals 1d8 necrotic damage and prevents them from healing for 1 turn. (Cost: 1 AP, 5 MP)
2. **Ray of Sickness:** Fire a green beam. Deals 2d8 poison damage and target must make a RES save or be poisoned. (Cost: 1 AP, 10 MP)
3. **Inflict Wounds:** Touch attack dealing a massive 3d10 necrotic damage. (Cost: 1 AP, 15 MP)

### **Level 3 Abilities (Choose 1):**
1. **Animate Dead:** Raise a skeleton or zombie from a medium or small corpse. (Cost: 1 AP, 20 MP)
2. **Ray of Enfeeblement:** A black beam saps the target's strength. Their melee attacks deal half damage for 1 minute (RES save negates). (Cost: 1 AP, 15 MP)
3. **Blindness/Deafness:** Target makes a RES save or goes completely blind or deaf for 1 minute. (Cost: 1 AP, 15 MP)

### **Level 5 Abilities (Choose 1):**
1. **Vampiric Touch:** Touch attack dealing 3d6 necrotic damage. You heal HP equal to the damage dealt. (Cost: 1 AP, 20 MP)
2. **Speak with Dead:** Temporarily bring a spirit back to its corpse to answer 5 questions truthfully. (Cost: 1 AP, 15 MP)
3. **Animate Abomination:** Merge three corpses together into a massive Flesh Golem that fights for you. (Cost: 2 AP, 40 MP)

### **Level 7 Abilities (Choose 1):**
1. **Blight:** Drain the moisture and vitality from a target, dealing 8d8 necrotic damage. (Cost: 1 AP, 35 MP)
2. **Death Ward:** Protect a target. The next time they would drop to 0 HP, they drop to 1 HP instead. (Cost: 1 AP, 30 MP)
3. **Summon Wraith:** Summon an incorporeal wraith that can fly, pass through walls, and drain levels. (Cost: 1 AP, 40 MP)

### **Level 9 Abilities (Choose 1):**
1. **Cloudkill:** A 20ft radius cloud of highly toxic gas. Deals 5d8 poison damage per turn to anyone inside. (Cost: 1 AP, 45 MP)
2. **Danse Macabre:** Instantly animate up to 5 corpses within 60ft as skeletons or zombies. They act immediately. (Cost: 1 AP, 50 MP)
3. **Negative Energy Flood:** Deal 5d12 necrotic damage in a 20ft area. Any creature killed by this instantly rises as a zombie under your control. (Cost: 1 AP, 50 MP)

### **Level 11 Abilities (Choose 1):**
1. **Circle of Death:** A sphere of negative energy ripples out in a 60ft radius, dealing 8d6 necrotic damage to everything. (Cost: 1 AP, 60 MP)
2. **Create Undead:** Raise powerful undead, such as Ghouls, Wights, or Mummies. (Cost: 2 AP, 65 MP)
3. **Magic Jar:** Project your soul out of your body and possess a humanoid target, taking full control of them. (Cost: 1 AP, 60 MP)

### **Level 13 Abilities (Choose 1):**
1. **Finger of Death:** Point at a creature. Deals 7d8 + 30 necrotic damage. If killed, they permanently become a zombie under your command. (Cost: 1 AP, 75 MP)
2. **Symbol of Death:** Scribe a rune on a surface. When triggered, everyone in 60ft takes 10d10 necrotic damage. (Cost: 1 AP, 70 MP)
3. **Soul Cage:** Trap the soul of a dying humanoid. You can exploit the soul to heal yourself, steal information, or gain advantage. (Cost: 1 AP, Reaction, 50 MP)

### **Level 15 Abilities (Choose 1):**
1. **Clone:** Grow an inert duplicate of yourself over 120 days. If you die, your soul instantly transfers to the clone. (Cost: 100 MP, Ritual)
2. **Abi-Dalzim's Horrid Wilting:** Draw the moisture from every creature in a 30ft cube, dealing 12d8 necrotic damage. (Cost: 1 AP, 85 MP)
3. **Summon Dracolich:** Raise the skeleton of a dragon to fight for you for 1 hour. It retains its breath weapon but deals necrotic damage. (Cost: 2 AP, 100 MP)

### **Level 17 Abilities (Choose 1):**
1. **Power Word Kill:** Utter a single word of death. If the target has less than 100 HP, they die instantly. (Cost: 1 AP, 120 MP)
2. **Weird:** Plunge a 30ft area into a nightmare dimension. Targets take 4d10 psychic damage per turn and are paralyzed by fear. (Cost: 1 AP, 110 MP)
3. **Plague of Undeath:** Every corpse within a 1-mile radius rises as a zombie under your command for 24 hours. (Cost: 3 AP, 150 MP)

### **Level 19 Abilities (Choose 1):**
1. **True Resurrection (Corrupted):** Bring anyone back to life from any state, but they return as a sentient, loyal Undead version of themselves. (Cost: 2 AP, 150 MP)
2. **Aura of Annihilation:** For 1 minute, anything you touch (including magical barriers or weapons) disintegrates into dust. (Cost: 2 AP, 130 MP)
3. **Lichdom:** Perform a ritual to permanently become a Lich. You gain immunity to non-magical damage and cannot die as long as your phylactery is intact. (Cost: Ritual)

### **Level 2 Skills (Choose 1):**
1. **Grim Harvest:** Once per turn, when you kill a creature with a spell, you heal HP equal to double the spell's level. (Passive)
2. **Corpse Collector:** You can shrink corpses and store up to 20 of them in your pockets for later use. (Passive)
3. **Undead Fortitude:** If damage reduces you to 0 HP, make a RES save. On a success, drop to 1 HP instead. (Passive)

### **Level 4 Skills (Choose 1):**
1. **Aura of Dread:** Enemies within 10ft of you have disadvantage on saving throws against fear. (Passive Aura)
2. **Flesh Crafter:** You can stitch extra limbs onto your undead minions, giving them additional attacks. (Passive)
3. **Grave Sight:** You have darkvision up to 120ft, and you can see invisible creatures or spirits. (Passive)

### **Level 6 Skills (Choose 1):**
1. **Undying Thralls:** Any undead you create gain Max HP equal to your Class Level + your INT score, and their weapon damage adds your INT mod. (Passive)
2. **Command Undead:** You can take control of undead created by other necromancers or naturally occurring in the wild (Will save). (Once/day)
3. **Necrotic Resistance:** You gain resistance to necrotic damage and your Max HP cannot be reduced. (Passive)

### **Level 8 Skills (Choose 1):**
1. **Soul Tether:** If an ally dies within 30ft of you, you can instantly trap their soul, preventing true death and allowing easy resurrection. (Passive, Reaction)
2. **Rotting Aura:** Any enemy that hits you with a melee attack takes 1d6 necrotic damage. (Passive)
3. **Bone Shield:** As a reaction, shatter a nearby bone to absorb 3d10 damage from an incoming attack. (Cost: 1 AP, Reaction)

### **Level 10 Skills (Choose 1):**
1. **Master of Death:** You can cast *Animate Dead* as a bonus action (0 AP). (Passive)
2. **Inured to Undeath:** You become completely immune to necrotic damage and cannot be poisoned or diseased. (Passive)
3. **Shadow Stride:** You can teleport between shadows up to 60ft away as a free action. (Once/day)

### **Level 12 Skills (Choose 1):**
1. **Life Siphon:** Whenever one of your undead minions deals damage, you heal for half the amount. (Passive)
2. **Exploding Corpses:** When one of your minions dies, it explodes, dealing 4d6 necrotic damage in a 10ft radius. (Passive)
3. **Ghostly Evasion:** You can move through solid objects and creatures as if they were difficult terrain. (Passive)

### **Level 14 Skills (Choose 1):**
1. **Deathly Pallor:** You no longer need to breathe, eat, or sleep. (Passive)
2. **Reaper's Scythe:** Your spell attacks and melee attacks ignore necrotic resistance and treat necrotic immunity as resistance. (Passive)
3. **Legion's Shield:** Any damage you take can be distributed evenly among all your active minions. (Passive)

### **Level 16 Skills (Choose 1):**
1. **Avatar of Death:** You can transform into a grim reaper figure, gaining a fly speed and dealing +4d10 necrotic damage on all attacks for 1 minute. (Once/day)
2. **Curse of Mortality:** Target loses all immunities, resistances, and regeneration for 1 hour. (Once/day)
3. **Grave Speech:** You can automatically understand and speak all languages, living or dead. (Passive)

### **Level 18 Skills (Choose 1):**
1. **Immortal Soul:** You literally cannot be killed by non-magical means. (Passive)
2. **Army of the Night:** The maximum number of undead you can control is multiplied by 10. (Passive)
3. **Death's Embrace:** Any enemy that drops to 0 HP within 60ft of you is permanently destroyed; their soul is consumed by you, restoring 50 MP. (Passive)

### **Level 20 Skills (Choose 1):**
1. **God of the Dead:** You gain the ability to kill any one mortal creature instantly, no saving throw, regardless of HP. (Once/month)
2. **The Black Gate:** Open a permanent portal to the Underworld, allowing spirits to pour through and do your bidding. (Once/day)
3. **Eternal Eclipse:** Plunge a 5-mile radius into absolute magical darkness for 24 hours. Undead in this area gain double HP and damage. (Once/day)
