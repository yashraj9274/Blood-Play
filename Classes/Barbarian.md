_"Fierce warriors fueled by primal rage and unyielding endurance."_
**Mana Dependency** : 1/5
## Class Focus (Single-Class Only)
_Granted at Character Creation. Lost if you multiclass. Choose ONE:_
1. **Blood Thirsty:** Deals 2x damage once after every time you hit a target's CHP.
2. **Unstoppable Force:** While raging, you are immune to all crowd-control (stun, root, prone) and your attacks ignore 50% of the target's Combined RN.
3. **Path of the Ancestors:** While raging, spectral warriors surround you, reducing all incoming damage by your Class Level.

### **Class Perk (Level 1): Rage**
- **Rage:** As a free action, enter a rage for 5 turns. Your Strength and Resistance modifiers are doubled. You cannot cast spells while raging. 
- You have advantage on Strength checks and Strength saving throws.
- Unarmored Defense: If you wear no armor, your base ARN is equal to `10 + RES mod + Agility mod`.

### **Bonus Perk (Level 10 - Single-Class Only): Primal Champion**
- Your Strength and Resistance scores can exceed the normal mortal cap of 20 (up to 24).
- If you drop to 0 HP while raging, you do not fall unconscious until your rage ends.

### **Level 1 Abilities (Choose 1):**
1. **Reckless Attack:** Gain advantage on all melee attack rolls this turn, but enemies have advantage against you until your next turn. (Cost: 0 AP)
2. **Cleave:** If you kill an enemy, you can immediately make another attack against an adjacent enemy. (Cost: 0 AP, Passive)
3. **Frightful Presence:** Let out a terrifying roar. Enemies within 15ft must make a Will save or be frightened. (Cost: 1 AP)

### **Level 3 Abilities (Choose 1):**
1. **Savage Leap:** Jump up to 30ft into the air and slam down, knocking enemies prone. (Cost: 1 AP)
2. **Bear Hug:** Grapple a target with massive force, dealing 1d10 + STR damage every turn they remain grappled. (Cost: 1 AP)
3. **Danger Sense:** You have advantage on Reflex saves against effects you can see (like traps or spells). (Passive)

### **Level 5 Abilities (Choose 1):**
1. **Extra Attack:** Make two attacks when you take the Attack action. (Cost: 1 AP)
2. **Feral Instinct:** You have advantage on Initiative rolls and can never be surprised. (Passive)
3. **Throw Anything:** You can use an enemy or heavy object as a thrown weapon, dealing 2d8 bludgeoning damage. (Cost: 1 AP)

### **Level 7 Abilities (Choose 1):**
1. **Mindless Rage:** While raging, you cannot be charmed or frightened. (Passive)
2. **Brutal Critical:** Roll an additional damage die when you critically hit. (Passive)
3. **Earthshaker:** Strike the ground, creating a tremor in a 20ft radius that deals 2d6 bludgeoning damage and knocks enemies prone. (Cost: 1 AP)

### **Level 9 Abilities (Choose 1):**
1. **Relentless Rage:** If you drop to 0 HP, make a RES save. On a success, you drop to 1 HP instead. (Passive)
2. **Intimidating Strike:** Hit a target so hard they lose their next AP. (Cost: 1 AP)
3. **Gore:** If you move at least 20ft straight toward a target, your attack deals an extra 3d6 damage. (Cost: 1 AP)

### **Level 11 Abilities (Choose 1):**
1. **Zealous Presence:** Let out a battle cry. You and all allies within 30ft gain advantage on attacks for 1 round. (Cost: 1 AP)
2. **Retaliation:** When you take damage from a creature within reach, you can make a melee attack against them. (Cost: 1 AP, Reaction)
3. **Ignore Pain:** Gain 50 Temporary AHP that lasts until your rage ends. (Cost: 1 AP)

### **Level 13 Abilities (Choose 1):**
1. **Brutal Critical II:** Roll two additional damage dice on a critical hit. (Passive)
2. **Sunder:** Destroy an enemy's shield or severely dent their armor, permanently lowering their ARN by 5. (Cost: 1 AP)
3. **Ancestral Shield:** Protect an ally. Any damage they take is transferred to you instead, reduced by half. (Passive Aura)

### **Level 15 Abilities (Choose 1):**
1. **Persistent Rage:** Your rage only ends if you fall unconscious or choose to end it. (Passive)
2. **Whirlwind Strike:** Attack every enemy within reach with a single sweeping blow. (Cost: 1 AP)
3. **Bloodlust:** Healing magic cast on you heals for double the amount while you are below 50% HP. (Passive)

### **Level 17 Abilities (Choose 1):**
1. **God of War:** While raging, your attacks ignore all physical immunities and resistances. (Passive)
2. **Meteor Leap:** Jump up to 100ft and crash down like a meteor, dealing 8d10 damage in a 30ft radius. (Cost: 2 AP)
3. **Indomitable Might:** If your total for a Strength check is less than your Strength Score, you can use your Score instead. (Passive)

### **Level 19 Abilities (Choose 1):**
1. **Primal Champion:** Your Strength and Resistance increase by +4, raising your maximums to 28. (Passive)
2. **Executioner:** If an enemy is below 20% HP, your next attack against them instantly kills them. (Cost: 1 AP)
3. **Avatar of Destruction:** You grow one size category. Your weapons deal double damage dice. (Passive)

### **Level 2 Skills (Choose 1):**
1. **Fast Movement:** Your speed increases by +10ft while you aren't wearing heavy armor. (Passive)
2. **Survivalist:** Advantage on all tracking, hunting, and nature checks. (Passive)
3. **Thick Hide:** Gain +1 to your Resistance modifier. (Passive)

### **Level 4 Skills (Choose 1):**
1. **Instinctive Dodge:** As a reaction, halve the damage of an incoming attack. (Once/day)
2. **Roar of Triumph:** Upon defeating an enemy, heal yourself for 2d8 HP. (Passive)
3. **Mighty Heave:** You can lift, push, and drag objects that weigh up to 5 tons. (Passive)

### **Level 6 Skills (Choose 1):**
1. **Animal Spirit:** Gain the ability to communicate with beasts. (Passive)
2. **Unflinching:** You cannot be knocked prone by any non-magical means. (Passive)
3. **Blood Tracker:** You have advantage tracking any creature that is currently bleeding or missing HP. (Passive)

### **Level 8 Skills (Choose 1):**
1. **Iron Grip:** You can wield a two-handed weapon in one hand. (Passive)
2. **Feral Leap:** Your jump distance is tripled. (Passive)
3. **Battle Scars:** For every 10% HP you are missing, gain +1 to your damage rolls. (Passive)

### **Level 10 Skills (Choose 1):**
1. **Terrifying Glare:** You can freeze an enemy in fear with a look. They skip their next turn. (Once/day)
2. **Unstoppable Charge:** Move in a straight line up to your speed. Everything in your path takes 4d6 damage and is shoved aside. (Once/day)
3. **Primal Vigor:** Regain HP equal to your max HP over the course of 1 minute outside of combat. (Once/day)

### **Level 12 Skills (Choose 1):**
1. **Spirit Walker:** You can cast the *Commune with Nature* spell without using MP. (Once/day)
2. **Toughness:** Your Max HP increases by +30 permanently. (Passive)
3. **Adrenaline Rush:** Ignore all levels of exhaustion and fatigue for 1 hour. (Once/day)

### **Level 14 Skills (Choose 1):**
1. **Roar of the Ancients:** Give all allies within 60ft immunity to fear and +2d6 damage to their attacks for 1 minute. (Once/day)
2. **Unbreakable:** Gain resistance to all magical damage types (fire, cold, etc.) for 1 minute. (Once/day)
3. **Colossus Slayer:** Your attacks deal an extra 2d8 damage against creatures larger than you. (Passive)

### **Level 16 Skills (Choose 1):**
1. **Death Defier:** If an attack would kill you outright, you instead drop to 1 HP and immediately enter a Rage. (Once/day)
2. **Heart of the Wild:** You become immune to poison and disease permanently. (Passive)
3. **Earthquake:** Stomp the ground, causing a localized earthquake that destroys buildings and knocks enemies prone in a 100ft radius. (Once/day)

### **Level 18 Skills (Choose 1):**
1. **Mythic Resilience:** You take half damage from all sources while raging. (Passive)
2. **Slayer of Gods:** Critical hits against divine, demonic, or celestial beings deal x4 damage. (Passive)
3. **Blood Frenzy:** You can take a 3rd action on your turn, but you take 20 damage at the end of the turn. (Once/day)

### **Level 20 Skills (Choose 1):**
1. **Immortal Rage:** While raging, your HP cannot drop below 1. (Once/day for 1 minute)
2. **World Breaker:** You can completely shatter a magical barrier, mountain, or fortress with a single punch. (Once/day)
3. **Apex Predator:** You automatically critically hit targets that have not yet taken a turn in combat. (Passive)
