_"Divine conduits of the Gods, wielding miracles of healing and holy wrath."_
**Mana Dependency** : 4/5
## Class Focus (Single-Class Only)
_Granted at Character Creation. Lost if you multiclass. Choose ONE:_
1. **Divine Intervention:** Any healing you output that exceeds a target's Max HP is converted into an impenetrable Ward (Temporary AHP) that lasts until depleted.
2. **Righteous Fury:** Your melee weapon attacks deal bonus Radiant damage equal to your Intelligence modifier x 2.
3. **Martyr's Grace:** You can automatically succeed any failed saving throw by sacrificing 10% of your Max HP.

### **Class Perk (Level 1): Spellcasting (Divine)**
- You use Intelligence as your spellcasting modifier.
- You can prepare and cast divine spells from the Cleric spell list using MP.
- You gain proficiency with heavy armor, shields, and simple weapons.

### **Bonus Perk (Level 10 - Single-Class Only): Chosen of the Gods**
- Your healing spells automatically roll their maximum possible value.
- You can channel your deity's power once per week to cast the *Miracle* spell (acts as a Wish, but tied to your God's domain).

### **Level 1 Abilities (Choose 1):**
1. **Cure Wounds:** Touch an ally to heal them for 1d8 + INT. (Cost: 1 AP, 10 MP)
2. **Guiding Bolt:** Fire a beam of light. Deals 4d6 radiant damage and the next attack against the target has advantage. (Cost: 1 AP, 15 MP)
3. **Shield of Faith:** Grant an ally +2 AC and +5 ARN for 10 minutes. (Cost: 1 AP, 10 MP)

### **Level 3 Abilities (Choose 1):**
1. **Spiritual Weapon:** Summon a floating, spectral weapon that attacks an enemy for 1d8 + INT force damage. (Cost: 1 AP, 15 MP)
2. **Hold Person:** Paralyze a humanoid target (Will save negates). (Cost: 1 AP, 20 MP)
3. **Prayer of Healing:** Heal up to 6 allies within 30ft for 2d8 + INT. (Cost: 1 AP, 25 MP)

### **Level 5 Abilities (Choose 1):**
1. **Spirit Guardians:** Spectral angels surround you. Enemies starting their turn within 15ft take 3d8 radiant damage and their speed is halved. (Cost: 1 AP, 30 MP)
2. **Revivify:** Bring an ally who died within the last minute back to life with 1 HP. (Cost: 1 AP, 50 MP)
3. **Mass Healing Word:** Heal up to 6 allies within 60ft for 1d4 + INT as a quick word. (Cost: 1 AP, 20 MP)

### **Level 7 Abilities (Choose 1):**
1. **Banishment:** Send an extraplanar creature back to its home plane, or send a native creature to a harmless demiplane for 1 minute (Will save). (Cost: 1 AP, 40 MP)
2. **Death Ward:** Protect a target. The next time they would drop to 0 HP, they drop to 1 HP instead. (Cost: 1 AP, 30 MP)
3. **Guardian of Faith:** Summon a large spectral guardian that strikes enemies who come near for 20 radiant damage. (Cost: 1 AP, 35 MP)

### **Level 9 Abilities (Choose 1):**
1. **Flame Strike:** Call down a pillar of divine fire dealing 4d6 fire and 4d6 radiant damage in a 10ft radius. (Cost: 1 AP, 45 MP)
2. **Mass Cure Wounds:** Heal up to 6 allies for 3d8 + INT. (Cost: 1 AP, 50 MP)
3. **Holy Contagion:** Afflict an enemy with a divine disease that rots their flesh, causing them to take double damage from all sources for 3 turns. (Cost: 1 AP, 40 MP)

### **Level 11 Abilities (Choose 1):**
1. **Heal:** Touch an ally to instantly heal them for 70 HP and cure all blindness, deafness, and diseases. (Cost: 1 AP, 60 MP)
2. **Blade Barrier:** Create a wall of whirling radiant blades that deals 6d10 slashing damage to anyone passing through. (Cost: 1 AP, 55 MP)
3. **Word of Recall:** Instantly teleport yourself and up to 5 allies back to a designated sanctuary (like a temple). (Cost: 1 AP, 60 MP)

### **Level 13 Abilities (Choose 1):**
1. **Divine Word:** Utter a holy word that instantly kills, blinds, or deafens creatures based on their remaining HP. (Cost: 1 AP, 70 MP)
2. **Resurrection:** Bring a dead creature back to life with full HP and remove all curses, provided they died within a century. (Cost: 2 AP, 100 MP)
3. **Fire Storm:** Create a massive storm of holy fire dealing 7d10 fire damage in a 100ft area. (Cost: 1 AP, 70 MP)

### **Level 15 Abilities (Choose 1):**
1. **Holy Aura:** You and allies within 30ft gain advantage on all saving throws, and enemies have disadvantage to hit you. (Cost: 1 AP, 80 MP)
2. **Antimagic Field:** Create a 10ft sphere where NO magic can function (spells fizzle, magic items become mundane). (Cost: 1 AP, 80 MP)
3. **Earthquake:** Cause a massive tremor in a 100ft radius, destroying structures and opening fissures. (Cost: 1 AP, 75 MP)

### **Level 17 Abilities (Choose 1):**
1. **Mass Heal:** A flood of healing energy restores 700 HP, divided as you choose among any number of allies in 60ft. (Cost: 1 AP, 120 MP)
2. **Gate:** Open a portal to a specific location on another plane of existence, or summon a specific entity to you. (Cost: 1 AP, 100 MP)
3. **Wrath of God:** Call down the ultimate divine punishment. Deal 20d10 radiant damage to a single target; if it dies, its soul is destroyed. (Cost: 2 AP, 150 MP)

### **Level 19 Abilities (Choose 1):**
1. **True Resurrection:** Bring anyone back to life from any state, even if their body was completely destroyed. (Cost: 2 AP, 200 MP)
2. **Storm of Vengeance:** Summon a gargantuan acidic, lightning-filled storm that wreaks havoc over a 360ft radius for 10 turns. (Cost: 2 AP, 150 MP)
3. **Divine Avatar:** Transform into an angelic being for 1 minute. You gain a fly speed, immunity to physical damage, and all your spells cost 0 MP. (Cost: 1 AP, 100 MP)

### **Level 2 Skills (Choose 1):**
1. **Turn Undead:** Undead within 30ft must make a Will save or run away from you in terror. (Once/day)
2. **Blessed Healer:** Whenever you cast a healing spell on another creature, you regain HP equal to 2 + the spell's level. (Passive)
3. **Divine Insight:** Gain advantage on all Insight and Religion checks. (Passive)

### **Level 4 Skills (Choose 1):**
1. **Channel Divinity:** Restore 30 MP to yourself or an ally through prayer. (Once/day)
2. **Aura of Life:** Allies within 30ft cannot have their Max HP reduced and gain resistance to necrotic damage. (Passive)
3. **Holy Vigor:** Your Max HP increases by +Class Level x 5. (Passive)

### **Level 6 Skills (Choose 1):**
1. **Destroy Undead:** When you use Turn Undead, any undead that fails the save and has less than 20 HP is instantly destroyed. (Passive)
2. **Potent Spellcasting:** Add your INT modifier to the damage you deal with any Cleric spell. (Passive)
3. **Divine Strike:** Once on each of your turns when you hit with a weapon, deal an extra 1d8 radiant damage. (Passive)

### **Level 8 Skills (Choose 1):**
1. **Supreme Healing:** When you normally roll one or more dice to restore hit points, you instead use the highest number possible. (Once/day for 1 minute)
2. **Beacon of Hope:** Allies within 30ft have advantage on Will saves and death saves. (Passive)
3. **Armor of Light:** Your heavy armor shines, blinding any melee attacker who hits you (RES save negates). (Passive)

### **Level 10 Skills (Choose 1):**
1. **Divine Intervention (Minor):** Call upon your deity to immediately end one negative effect, spell, or curse on the party. (Once/day)
2. **Aura of Purity:** You and allies within 30ft are immune to disease and have advantage on saves against poison. (Passive)
3. **Cleansing Touch:** Touch a creature to automatically end any charm, fear, or petrification effect on them. (Once/day)

### **Level 12 Skills (Choose 1):**
1. **Saintly Endurance:** You gain resistance to bludgeoning, piercing, and slashing damage from non-magical weapons. (Passive)
2. **Halo:** A permanent glowing halo grants you 60ft of darkvision and advantage on Intimidation and Seduction checks against evil creatures. (Passive)
3. **Vow of Silence:** You can cast spells without needing to speak any verbal components. (Passive)

### **Level 14 Skills (Choose 1):**
1. **Corona of Light:** Enemies within 30ft of you have disadvantage on saving throws against spells that deal fire or radiant damage. (Passive)
2. **Resilient Spirit:** You automatically succeed on all Intelligence saving throws. (Passive)
3. **Angelic Wings:** You sprout a pair of ethereal wings, gaining a fly speed of 30ft. (Passive)

### **Level 16 Skills (Choose 1):**
1. **Ascended Healing:** Your healing spells can revive characters who have died within the last round without needing a resurrection spell. (Passive)
2. **God's Shield:** You create an impenetrable wall of light that blocks all physical and magical attacks for 1 round. (Once/day)
3. **Holy Smite:** Your weapon attacks now permanently deal +3d8 radiant damage. (Passive)

### **Level 18 Skills (Choose 1):**
1. **Aura of the Divine:** Allies within 30ft of you cannot drop below 1 HP while you are conscious. (Passive)
2. **Prophet's Vision:** You can see the future, meaning you can never be surprised and attacks against you always have disadvantage. (Passive)
3. **Miracle Worker:** You can cast one spell of any level without expending any MP. (Once/day)

### **Level 20 Skills (Choose 1):**
1. **Ascension:** You become an immortal celestial. You are immune to aging, disease, poison, and necrotic damage. (Passive)
2. **Rapture:** You can instantly teleport yourself and up to 10 willing creatures to the divine realm of your deity. (Once/day)
3. **God's Voice:** You speak a command that reality itself must obey. You dictate one event to happen exactly as you wish. (Once/week)
