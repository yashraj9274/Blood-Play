_"Conjurors of otherworldly beasts, demons, and celestial guardians."_
**Mana Dependency** : 4/5
## Class Focus (Single-Class Only)
_Granted at Character Creation. Lost if you multiclass. Choose ONE:_
1. **Convergence:** You can fuse two of your summons together, creating a massive entity that combines both of their HP, damage dice, and special abilities.
2. **Swarm Tactics:** The maximum number of summons you can maintain simultaneously is increased by 3.
3. **Eidolon Link:** Your primary summon shares your HP pool and your Resistance Modifier, making both of you incredibly tanky.

### **Class Perk (Level 1): Summoning Magic**
- You use Intelligence as your spellcasting modifier.
- You can summon creatures from other planes (Fey, Fiends, Elementals, Celestials) using MP.
- Summons act on your turn and require a free action to command.

### **Bonus Perk (Level 10 - Single-Class Only): Master Conjuror**
- Your summons gain bonus Max HP equal to your Class Level x 10.
- If your primary summon dies, it explodes, dealing its Max HP as force damage to everything within 20ft.

### **Level 1 Abilities (Choose 1):**
1. **Summon Familiar:** Summon a tiny spirit (owl, cat, snake) to act as a scout. You can see through its eyes. (Cost: 1 AP, 10 MP)
2. **Summon Elemental Spirit:** Summon a small elemental (Fire, Water, Earth, Air) that deals 1d6 elemental damage. (Cost: 1 AP, 15 MP)
3. **Conjure Weapon:** Summon a magical sword or bow directly into your hands that uses your INT for attacks. (Cost: 1 AP, 5 MP)

### **Level 3 Abilities (Choose 1):**
1. **Summon Lesser Demon:** Summon 1d4 minor demons. They are hostile to everyone, including you, unless you pass a Will save to control them. (Cost: 1 AP, 20 MP)
2. **Flock of Familiars:** Summon 3 flying familiars that distract enemies, granting allies advantage on attacks. (Cost: 1 AP, 15 MP)
3. **Banishment Swap:** Teleport yourself, swapping places with one of your summons up to 60ft away. (Cost: 1 AP, 10 MP)

### **Level 5 Abilities (Choose 1):**
1. **Summon Elemental:** Summon a Large elemental. It has 50 HP and deals 2d8 damage per hit. (Cost: 1 AP, 30 MP)
2. **Conjure Animals:** Summon 2 Dire Wolves or 4 Giant Spiders to fight for you. (Cost: 1 AP, 30 MP)
3. **Misty Step:** Teleport up to 30ft to an unoccupied space you can see. (Cost: 0 AP, Bonus, 15 MP)

### **Level 7 Abilities (Choose 1):**
1. **Summon Greater Demon:** Summon a massive demon (like a Barlgura). It hits for 3d10 damage but requires a Will save every turn to control. (Cost: 1 AP, 40 MP)
2. **Dimension Door:** Teleport yourself and one ally up to 500ft in any direction. (Cost: 1 AP, 35 MP)
3. **Conjure Minor Elementals:** Summon 4 Magmins or Ice Mephits that explode upon death. (Cost: 1 AP, 35 MP)

### **Level 9 Abilities (Choose 1):**
1. **Summon Celestial:** Summon an angelic being. It can fly, heal allies for 2d8, and attack with radiant weapons. (Cost: 1 AP, 50 MP)
2. **Wall of Stone:** Conjure a permanent 6-inch thick wall of solid stone to block enemies or create bridges. (Cost: 1 AP, 40 MP)
3. **Insect Plague:** A massive 20ft radius swarm of locusts deals 4d10 piercing damage and heavily obscures the area. (Cost: 1 AP, 45 MP)

### **Level 11 Abilities (Choose 1):**
1. **Summon Fey:** Summon a powerful Fey spirit that can charm enemies, turn invisible, and deal psychic damage. (Cost: 1 AP, 60 MP)
2. **Planar Ally:** Beseech a god or powerful entity to send a specific, highly intelligent creature to help you for a steep price. (Cost: 1 AP, 70 MP)
3. **Arcane Gate:** Create two linked portals up to 500ft apart that stay open for 10 minutes. (Cost: 1 AP, 55 MP)

### **Level 13 Abilities (Choose 1):**
1. **Summon Draconic Spirit:** Summon the spirit of a Large Dragon. It has a breath weapon dealing 6d8 damage and flies. (Cost: 1 AP, 75 MP)
2. **Forcecage:** Trap a creature in an invisible, unbreakable box of force magic for 1 hour. (Cost: 1 AP, 70 MP)
3. **Teleport:** Instantly transport yourself and up to 8 allies to any location on the same plane of existence. (Cost: 1 AP, 80 MP)

### **Level 15 Abilities (Choose 1):**
1. **Maze:** Banish a target into a complex demiplanar labyrinth. They must pass a massive INT check to escape, or be trapped for 10 minutes. (Cost: 1 AP, 80 MP)
2. **Summon Fiend:** Summon a Pit Fiend or Balor. It has 200 HP and deals devastating fire/slashing damage. (Cost: 2 AP, 100 MP)
3. **Clone:** Grow an inert duplicate of yourself. If you die, your soul instantly transfers to the clone. (Cost: 100 MP, Ritual)

### **Level 17 Abilities (Choose 1):**
1. **Gate:** Open a portal to a specific location on another plane of existence, or forcefully summon a specific entity (even a god's avatar) to you. (Cost: 1 AP, 100 MP)
2. **Meteor Swarm:** Call down four massive meteors, each dealing 20d6 fire and 20d6 bludgeoning damage in a 40ft radius. (Cost: 2 AP, 150 MP)
3. **Summon Titan:** Summon an ancient Earth Titan. It is Gargantuan, has 500 HP, and its attacks cause earthquakes. (Cost: 2 AP, 120 MP)

### **Level 19 Abilities (Choose 1):**
1. **True Polymorph:** Permanently turn yourself, an ally, or an enemy into any creature or object of equal or lower CR. (Cost: 2 AP, 150 MP)
2. **Wish:** Cast the most powerful spell in reality. Alter the fabric of the universe to your whim. (Cost: 2 AP, 200 MP)
3. **Legion of the Planes:** Open rifts to the Fire, Water, Earth, and Air planes simultaneously, summoning four Elemental Lords at once. (Cost: 3 AP, 200 MP)

### **Level 2 Skills (Choose 1):**
1. **Durable Summons:** Any creature you summon automatically gains +10 Temporary AHP. (Passive)
2. **Planar Linguist:** You can speak, read, and write Abyssal, Celestial, Primordial, and Sylvan. (Passive)
3. **Pocket Dimension:** You can store up to 500 lbs of gear in an invisible extra-dimensional space. (Passive)

### **Level 4 Skills (Choose 1):**
1. **Potent Conjuration:** Your summons add your Proficiency Bonus to their attack and damage rolls. (Passive)
2. **Resilient Summons:** Your summons gain resistance to non-magical bludgeoning, piercing, and slashing damage. (Passive)
3. **Familiar's Bond:** You can cast touch spells through your familiar. (Passive)

### **Level 6 Skills (Choose 1):**
1. **Benign Transposition:** You can teleport up to 30ft as a bonus action, or swap places with a willing ally or summon. (Once/day)
2. **Elemental Absorption:** Gain resistance to Fire, Cold, or Lightning damage (choose after a long rest). (Passive)
3. **Speedy Summons:** Your summons gain +10ft to their movement speed and have advantage on initiative rolls. (Passive)

### **Level 8 Skills (Choose 1):**
1. **Focused Mind:** You have advantage on all Will saving throws and concentration checks. (Passive)
2. **Explosive Arrival:** When you summon a creature, it arrives in a burst of energy, dealing 2d6 damage to enemies within 5ft. (Passive)
3. **Aura of the Conjuror:** Allies within 15ft of you gain +2 to their AC. (Passive Aura)

### **Level 10 Skills (Choose 1):**
1. **Masterful Control:** You never lose control of summoned Demons or unpredictable entities, regardless of Will saves. (Passive)
2. **Twin Summon:** Whenever you cast a spell that summons one creature, you summon two identical creatures instead. (Passive)
3. **Planar Shield:** As a reaction, you can banish an incoming projectile or spell into another plane, taking 0 damage. (Once/day)

### **Level 12 Skills (Choose 1):**
1. **Arcane Recovery:** Regain half your Max MP when you take a short rest. (Once/day)
2. **Summoner's Vigor:** Whenever you summon a creature, you heal yourself for 2d10 HP. (Passive)
3. **Phase Shift:** You can move through solid objects and creatures as if they were difficult terrain. (Passive)

### **Level 14 Skills (Choose 1):**
1. **Eternal Summons:** Your summons no longer have a time limit; they remain until destroyed or dismissed. (Passive)
2. **Symbiotic Magic:** If you cast a buff spell on yourself, it automatically applies to all your active summons as well. (Passive)
3. **Iron Will:** You are completely immune to being charmed, frightened, or mind-controlled. (Passive)

### **Level 16 Skills (Choose 1):**
1. **Avatar of Conjuration:** You can summon any creature without expending MP. (Once/day)
2. **Perfect Synergy:** You and your summons cannot be critically hit, and you all have advantage on saving throws against magic. (Passive)
3. **Dimensional Anchor:** Enemies within 60ft of you cannot teleport or travel between planes. (Passive)

### **Level 18 Skills (Choose 1):**
1. **Immortal Summons:** If one of your summons drops to 0 HP, it instantly revives at full HP. (Once/day per summon)
2. **Planar Monarch:** You are recognized as royalty by Elementals, Celestials, and Fiends. They will not attack you unless provoked. (Passive)
3. **God-Tier Familiar:** Your familiar becomes a CR 10 creature (like a Young Dragon or powerful Demon) permanently. (Passive)

### **Level 20 Skills (Choose 1):**
1. **Army of the Cosmos:** You can summon 100 lesser elementals or demons at once to fight a massive war. (Once/week)
2. **Ascension:** You become an extraplanar entity. You stop aging, are immune to all non-magical damage, and can fly perfectly. (Passive)
3. **Absolute Fusion:** You can permanently fuse yourself with a summoned Titan or Celestial, gaining all of its stats and abilities on top of your own. (Once/lifetime)
