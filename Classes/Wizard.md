_"Scholars of the arcane arts, wielding a vast spellbook of ultimate cosmic power."_
**Mana Dependency** : 5/5
## Class Focus (Single-Class Only)
_Granted at Character Creation. Lost if you multiclass. Choose ONE:_
1. **Arcane Supremacy:** Spells you cast ignore magical resistances and immunities. (If a target is immune to Fire, they take full Fire damage anyway).
2. **Spell Sculptor:** You can perfectly shape your AoE spells so that allies inside the radius take zero damage and suffer no negative effects.
3. **Absolute Recall:** You can prepare and cast ANY spell in your spellbook at any time; you are no longer limited to a set number of prepared spells per day.

### **Class Perk (Level 1): Spellbook & Arcane Recovery**
- You use Intelligence as your spellcasting modifier.
- You have the largest MP pool of any class and can learn spells by copying scrolls into your spellbook.
- You regain half your Max MP upon completing a short rest.

### **Bonus Perk (Level 10 - Single-Class Only): Archmage**
- You can cast any Level 1 or Level 2 spell in your spellbook for 0 MP at will.
- You have advantage on saving throws against all magical effects.

### **Level 1 Abilities (Choose 1):**
1. **Magic Missile:** Fire 3 glowing darts of magical force. They automatically hit for 1d4+1 damage each. (Cost: 1 AP, 10 MP)
2. **Burning Hands:** A 15ft cone of fire shoots from your hands dealing 3d6 fire damage. (Cost: 1 AP, 10 MP)
3. **Shield:** An invisible barrier of magical force appears and protects you. Gain +5 AC until your next turn. (Cost: 1 AP, Reaction, 10 MP)

### **Level 3 Abilities (Choose 1):**
1. **Scorching Ray:** Fire three rays of fire. Make an attack roll for each. Each ray deals 2d6 fire damage. (Cost: 1 AP, 15 MP)
2. **Web:** Conjure a mass of thick, sticky webbing in a 20ft cube. Restrains enemies caught in it. (Cost: 1 AP, 15 MP)
3. **Misty Step:** Teleport up to 30ft to an unoccupied space you can see. (Cost: 0 AP, Bonus, 15 MP)

### **Level 5 Abilities (Choose 1):**
1. **Fireball:** A massive explosion of fire deals 8d6 fire damage in a 20ft radius. (Cost: 1 AP, 25 MP)
2. **Counterspell:** Interrupt a creature in the process of casting a spell, completely negating it. (Cost: 1 AP, Reaction, 20 MP)
3. **Haste:** Grant an ally double speed, +2 AC, advantage on DEX saves, and an extra action every turn. (Cost: 1 AP, 25 MP)

### **Level 7 Abilities (Choose 1):**
1. **Polymorph:** Transform an enemy into a harmless sheep, or an ally into a Giant Ape for 1 hour. (Cost: 1 AP, 40 MP)
2. **Dimension Door:** Teleport yourself and one ally up to 500ft in any direction. (Cost: 1 AP, 35 MP)
3. **Wall of Fire:** Create a blazing wall of fire that deals 5d8 fire damage to anyone passing through it. (Cost: 1 AP, 35 MP)

### **Level 9 Abilities (Choose 1):**
1. **Cone of Cold:** A blast of cold air deals 8d8 cold damage in a 60ft cone. (Cost: 1 AP, 45 MP)
2. **Wall of Force:** Create an invisible, absolutely unbreakable wall of magical force. (Cost: 1 AP, 40 MP)
3. **Telekinesis:** Move creatures or objects up to 1,000 lbs with your mind. (Cost: 1 AP, 40 MP)

### **Level 11 Abilities (Choose 1):**
1. **Disintegrate:** Fire a green ray. Deals 10d6 + 40 force damage. If this drops the target to 0 HP, they turn to dust. (Cost: 1 AP, 55 MP)
2. **Chain Lightning:** Fire a bolt of lightning that deals 10d8 damage to the target and leaps to 3 other targets. (Cost: 1 AP, 60 MP)
3. **Globe of Invulnerability:** Create a 10ft barrier around yourself that completely blocks all spells cast from outside it. (Cost: 1 AP, 50 MP)

### **Level 13 Abilities (Choose 1):**
1. **Teleport:** Instantly transport yourself and up to 8 allies to any location on the same plane of existence. (Cost: 1 AP, 80 MP)
2. **Delayed Blast Fireball:** Create a fireball that grows in power (1d6 per turn) until you choose to detonate it. Base 12d6 fire damage. (Cost: 1 AP, 75 MP)
3. **Reverse Gravity:** Reverse gravity in a 50ft radius cylinder, sending enemies falling upwards 100ft into the sky. (Cost: 1 AP, 75 MP)

### **Level 15 Abilities (Choose 1):**
1. **Maze:** Banish a target into a complex demiplanar labyrinth. They must pass a massive INT check to escape, or be trapped. (Cost: 1 AP, 80 MP)
2. **Clone:** Grow an inert duplicate of yourself. If you die, your soul instantly transfers to the clone. (Cost: 100 MP, Ritual)
3. **Sunburst:** A massive flash of light deals 12d6 radiant damage in a 60ft radius and blinds all enemies. (Cost: 1 AP, 85 MP)

### **Level 17 Abilities (Choose 1):**
1. **Meteor Swarm:** Call down four massive meteors, each dealing 20d6 fire and 20d6 bludgeoning damage in a 40ft radius. (Cost: 2 AP, 150 MP)
2. **Time Stop:** Stop time entirely for 1d4 + 1 turns. You can move and set up spells freely during this time. (Cost: 2 AP, 120 MP)
3. **Prismatic Wall:** Create an indestructible, multi-layered wall of light. Anyone passing through takes 50d6 damage of various types and may be petrified or banished. (Cost: 1 AP, 110 MP)

### **Level 19 Abilities (Choose 1):**
1. **Wish:** Cast the most powerful spell in reality. Alter the fabric of the universe to your whim. (Cost: 2 AP, 200 MP)
2. **True Polymorph:** Permanently turn yourself, an ally, or an enemy into any creature or object of equal or lower CR. (Cost: 2 AP, 150 MP)
3. **Invulnerability:** For 10 minutes, you are completely immune to all damage from all sources. (Cost: 2 AP, 150 MP)

### **Level 2 Skills (Choose 1):**
1. **Arcane Ward:** Whenever you cast an Abjuration spell, you create a magical shield with HP equal to your INT mod + Level. (Passive)
2. **Minor Conjuration:** You can magically conjure any small, non-magical object (keys, tools, weapons) in your hand. (Passive)
3. **Illusionist's Sight:** You automatically detect optical illusions and holograms. (Passive)

### **Level 4 Skills (Choose 1):**
1. **Potent Cantrips:** Your basic 0-MP spells still deal half damage even if the target succeeds their saving throw. (Passive)
2. **Transmuter's Stone:** Create a magical stone that grants the holder darkvision, movement speed, or elemental resistance. (Passive)
3. **Keen Mind:** You have a perfect memory. You can perfectly recall anything you have seen or heard in the past month. (Passive)

### **Level 6 Skills (Choose 1):**
1. **Malleable Illusions:** You can alter the parameters of an illusion spell you have already cast as a free action. (Passive)
2. **Benign Transposition:** You can teleport up to 30ft as a bonus action, or swap places with a willing ally. (Once/day)
3. **Empowered Evocation:** Add your INT modifier to the damage roll of any Evocation spell you cast. (Passive)

### **Level 8 Skills (Choose 1):**
1. **Expert Divination:** Casting Divination spells costs you 0 MP. (Passive)
2. **Arcane Deflection:** When you are hit by an attack, you can use your reaction to gain +2 AC and +4 to all saves for that attack. (Passive)
3. **Spell Sniper:** Your spell attacks ignore half cover and three-quarters cover, and their range is doubled. (Passive)

### **Level 10 Skills (Choose 1):**
1. **Shapechanger:** You can cast *Polymorph* on yourself at will, without expending MP. (Passive)
2. **Overchannel:** When you cast a spell of 5th level or lower that deals damage, you can force it to deal maximum possible damage automatically. (Once/day)
3. **Durable Magic:** While you are maintaining concentration on a spell, you gain +2 AC and +2 to all saving throws. (Passive)

### **Level 12 Skills (Choose 1):**
1. **Illusory Reality:** You can make one inanimate object in your illusion become totally real for 1 minute. (Once/day)
2. **Master Enchanter:** When you cast an Enchantment spell, you can target two creatures instead of one. (Passive)
3. **Elemental Mastery:** Choose Fire, Cold, or Lightning. Your spells of that type ignore resistance. (Passive)

### **Level 14 Skills (Choose 1):**
1. **Spell Resistance:** You have advantage on saving throws against ALL spells, and you have resistance against damage from spells. (Passive)
2. **Master Transmuter:** You can completely destroy the physical properties of an object, or revive a dead creature. (Once/day)
3. **Command Undead:** You can take permanent control of an undead creature. (Once/day)

### **Level 16 Skills (Choose 1):**
1. **Arcane Recovery II:** You now regain ALL of your expended MP upon completing a short rest. (Passive)
2. **Perfect Mind:** You cannot be charmed, frightened, or put to sleep by magic. (Passive)
3. **Warp Reality:** You can cast *Teleport* or *Plane Shift* as a free action to escape danger instantly. (Once/day)

### **Level 18 Skills (Choose 1):**
1. **Spell Mastery:** Choose one Level 1 spell and one Level 2 spell. You can cast them at their lowest level without expending MP. (Passive)
2. **Immortal Scholar:** You stop aging and become immune to diseases and curses. (Passive)
3. **Omniscience:** You can cast *Foresight* on yourself without expending MP. (Once/day)

### **Level 20 Skills (Choose 1):**
1. **Signature Spells:** Choose two Level 3 spells. They are always prepared, and you can cast each once per short rest without expending MP. (Passive)
2. **God of Magic:** Your Intelligence score permanently becomes 30 (+10 modifier). (Passive)
3. **Arcane Ascension:** You no longer need a spellbook, components, or words to cast spells. You simply will them into existence. (Passive)
