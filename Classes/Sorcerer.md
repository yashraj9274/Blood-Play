# Sorcerer

_"Spellcasters with innate magical power flowing through their veins."_

---
**Mana Dependency** : 5/5

## Class Focus (Choose 1 at Character Creation)

### **1. Arcane Bloodline**

==Your spells cost 10% less MP (round down, minimum 1). When you take damage while concentrating on a spell, add your INT modifier to the concentration check.==

### **2. Sorcerer Soul

Your magic comes from ancient arcane lineage. When you cast a spell, you can choose to empower it by spending HP instead of MP at a 2:1 ratio (1 CHP+1 WHP = 1 MP). Spells cast this way cannot be interrupted or countered.
### **3. Elemental Affinity**

You gain +1 Proficiency in all elements.

## Skills (Even Class Levels)

### **Level 2 Skills (Choose 1):**

==**Mana Efficiency** (Utility)==

- ==All spell costs reduced by 2 MP (minimum 1)==
- ==Recover 5 MP when you kill an enemy with a spell==

**Spell Weave** (Utility)

- Once per long rest, cast a spell you don't know (must be a level you can cast)
- Make an INT check (DC = spell level × 5) to successfully cast it

**Arcane Shield** (Utility)

- As a reaction, spend up to 50 MP to reduce incoming damage by 2 per MP spent

---

### **Level 4 Skills (Choose 1):**

**Extended Range** (Utility)

- All your spell ranges are doubled
- Touch spells become 5-foot range

**Spell Persistence** (Utility)

- Spells with duration last 50% longer
- Concentration spells don't break when you take less than 10 damage

**Quick Cast** (Combat)

- Once per short rest, cast a spell as a bonus action instead of an action

---

### **Level 6 Skills (Choose 1):**

**Penetrating Magic** (Combat)

- Your spells ignores target's ARN

**Twin Spell** (Combat)

- Once per long rest, when you cast a single-target spell, target a second creature within 30 feet 

**Counterspell** (Combat)

- As a reaction attempt to counter enemies spell  (INT check DC = 10 + spell level)

---

## Abilities (Odd Class Levels) - All cost 1 AP

### **Level 1 Abilities (Choose 1):**

**Arcane Bolt** (Combat)

- Fire a magical bolt at one target within 60 feet
- Deals 1d8 + INT modifier force damage
- Automatically hits (no attack roll)

**Mana Barrier** (Utility)

- Create a shield that absorbs 15 damage
- Lasts until depleted or end of combat

**Cantrip Mastery** (Utility)

- Use a minor magical effect (light, prestidigitation, mage hand)
- Can be used at will without AP cost

---

### **Level 3 Abilities (Choose 1):**

**Spell Echo** (Combat)

- After casting a spell, it echoes next turn dealing 50% damage to the same target

**Damage Burst** (Combat)

- Your next spell deals +1d8 damage

**Quick Shield** (Utility)

- As a reaction, gain +2 AC until start of your next turn

---

### **Level 5 Abilities (Choose 1):**

**Chain Spell** (Combat)

- Your next spell chains to 1 additional target within 20 feet, dealing 50% damage

**Spell Steal** (Utility)

- Copy the last spell cast within 60 feet; you can cast it once within 1 minute

**Arcane Precision** (Combat)

- Your next spell attack automatically hits

---

### **Level 7 Abilities (Choose 1):**

**Piercing Spell** (Combat)

- Your next spell ignores all resistances and 50% of RN

**Mana Drain** (Combat)

- Target loses 15 MP (or 15 HP if non-caster); you gain 10 MP

**Spell Reflection** (Utility)

- As a reaction, reflect the next spell targeting you back at the caster

---

### **Level 9 Abilities (Choose 1):**

**Multi-Cast** (Combat)

- Cast the same spell twice in one turn (pay MP for both)

**Arcane Surge** (Combat)

- Your next spell deals maximum damage on all dice

**Temporal Shift** (Utility)

- Reroll your initiative; take the new result

---

### **Level 11 Abilities (Choose 1):**

**Spell Mastery** (Combat)

- Your next spell cannot be countered and automatically bypasses saves (half damage becomes full damage)

**Emergency Teleport** (Utility)

- Teleport up to 60 feet to a location you can see

**Arcane Immunity** (Utility)

- Gain immunity to one damage type for 1 minute

---

### **Level 13 Abilities (Choose 1):**

**Devastating Spell** (Combat)

- Your next spell deals +3d8 damage

**Spell Duplication** (Combat)

- Cast your last spell again without spending MP

**Reality Warp** (Utility)

- Reroll any one roll (yours or enemy's) that just occurred

---

### **Level 15 Abilities (Choose 1):**

**Arcane Explosion** (Combat)

- Deal 4d10 force damage in a 20-foot radius centered on you

**Phase Shift** (Utility)

- Become incorporeal for 1 round (immune to physical damage, can move through objects)

**Spell Cascade** (Combat)

- Your next 3 spells cost 50% less MP

---

### **Level 17 Abilities (Choose 1):**

**Power Word** (Combat)

- Speak a word of power: target below 50 HP falls unconscious for 1 minute (Will save DC = spell save DC)

**Arcane Nova** (Combat)

- Deal 5d10 force damage to all enemies within 30 feet

**Temporal Loop** (Utility)

- Take an extra turn immediately after this one

---

### **Level 19 Abilities (Choose 1):**

**Meteor Strike** (Combat)

- Call down a meteor dealing 6d10 fire + 6d10 bludgeoning damage in a 40-foot radius

**Perfect Spell** (Combat)

- Your next spell deals maximum damage and cannot miss or be resisted

**Arcane Transcendence** (Utility)

- For 1 minute, all spells cost 1 MP and you gain +5 to AC
