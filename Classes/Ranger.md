_"Wilderness survivalists and unmatched sharpshooters."_
**Mana Dependency** : 1/5
## Class Focus (Single-Class Only)
_Granted at Character Creation. Lost if you multiclass. Choose ONE:_
1. **Infinite Ammo & Proficiency:** You gain double proficiency with any projectile weapon (bows, guns), and you never run out of mundane ammunition. *(Note: This is Flint's selected focus)*
2. **Apex Predator:** You automatically critically hit targets that have not yet taken a turn in combat, and your initiative is doubled.
3. **True Hunter:** Your tracking cannot be fooled by magical means, and your ranged attacks completely ignore all forms of cover (including full cover and magical barriers).

### **Class Perk (Level 1): Favored Enemy & Terrain**
- Choose a Favored Enemy (e.g., Beasts, Dragons, Undead). You have advantage on tracking them, and your attacks deal +2 damage against them.
- Choose a Favored Terrain (e.g., Forest, Desert, Underdark). You cannot get lost here, and difficult terrain does not slow you.
- You gain proficiency with all simple/martial weapons, firearms, light armor, and medium armor.

### **Bonus Perk (Level 10 - Single-Class Only): Master Tracker**
- You can cast *Hunter's Mark* as a free action without expending MP.
- You become invisible while you remain motionless in natural terrain.

### **Level 1 Abilities (Choose 1):**
1. **Hunter's Mark:** Mark a target. You deal an extra 1d6 damage to it whenever you hit with a weapon attack. (Cost: 1 AP, 5 MP)
2. **Ensnaring Strike:** The next time you hit a creature with a weapon attack, thorny vines wrap around them, restraining them (STR save). (Cost: 0 AP, Bonus, 5 MP)
3. **Hail of Thorns:** The next time you hit with a ranged attack, the projectile explodes in a 5ft radius dealing 1d10 piercing damage. (Cost: 0 AP, Bonus, 5 MP)

### **Level 3 Abilities (Choose 1):**
1. **Pass without Trace:** A veil of shadows masks you and your allies. You all gain +10 to Stealth checks and leave no tracks. (Cost: 1 AP, 15 MP)
2. **Spike Growth:** The ground in a 20ft radius becomes covered in sharp thorns. Moving through it deals 2d4 piercing damage for every 5ft. (Cost: 1 AP, 15 MP)
3. **Silence:** Create a 20ft radius sphere of absolute silence. No sound can pass through, preventing verbal spellcasting. (Cost: 1 AP, 10 MP)

### **Level 5 Abilities (Choose 1):**
1. **Extra Attack:** Make two attacks when you take the Attack action. (Cost: 1 AP)
2. **Lightning Arrow:** Your next ranged attack turns into a bolt of lightning dealing 4d8 lightning damage, plus 2d8 to everyone within 10ft. (Cost: 0 AP, Bonus, 20 MP)
3. **Conjure Barrage:** Throw a weapon or fire an arrow that duplicates mid-air, creating a 60ft cone dealing 3d8 damage. (Cost: 1 AP, 20 MP)

### **Level 7 Abilities (Choose 1):**
1. **Escape the Horde:** Opportunity attacks against you are made with disadvantage. (Passive)
2. **Multiattack Defense:** When a creature hits you, you gain +4 AC against all subsequent attacks made by that creature for the rest of the turn. (Passive)
3. **Steel Will:** You have advantage on saving throws against being frightened or charmed. (Passive)

### **Level 9 Abilities (Choose 1):**
1. **Conjure Woodland Beings:** Summon fey creatures or dryads to fight for you for 1 hour. (Cost: 1 AP, 30 MP)
2. **Grasping Vine:** Conjure a 30ft vine that pulls an enemy 20ft toward you. (Cost: 1 AP, 25 MP)
3. **Volley:** You can use your action to make a ranged attack against any number of creatures within a 10ft radius of a point you can see. (Cost: 1 AP)

### **Level 11 Abilities (Choose 1):**
1. **Whirlwind Strike:** You can use your action to make a melee attack against any number of creatures within 5ft of you. (Cost: 1 AP)
2. **Stalker's Flurry:** Once on each of your turns, if you miss with a weapon attack, you can make another weapon attack as part of the same action. (Passive)
3. **Swift Quiver:** Your quiver magically replaces ammunition. You can make two extra ranged attacks as a bonus action on each of your turns. (Cost: 1 AP, 30 MP)

### **Level 13 Abilities (Choose 1):**
1. **Tree Stride:** Step into a tree and instantly teleport to any other tree of the same kind within 500ft. (Cost: 1 AP, 30 MP)
2. **Guardian of Nature:** Transform into a primal beast or tree form. Gain 10 temp HP, advantage on attacks, and your attacks deal +1d6 force damage. (Cost: 1 AP, 35 MP)
3. **Sniper's Blessing:** You do not require an Aim roll, and your ranged attacks deal 1.5x damage automatically. *(Note: Flint's selected ability)* (Cost: 1 AP, 20 MP)

### **Level 15 Abilities (Choose 1):**
1. **Evasion:** When subjected to an AoE effect, you take no damage on a successful save, and half on a fail. (Passive)
2. **Stand Against the Tide:** When a hostile creature misses you with a melee attack, you can force it to repeat the attack against another creature of your choice. (Reaction, Passive)
3. **Shadowy Dodge:** You can use your reaction to halve the damage of any incoming attack you can see. (Reaction, Passive)

### **Level 17 Abilities (Choose 1):**
1. **Conjure Volley:** Fire a single arrow that multiplies into a massive rain of arrows covering a 40ft radius, dealing 8d8 piercing damage. (Cost: 1 AP, 50 MP)
2. **Swift Death:** Your first attack against a creature in combat automatically hits and is treated as a critical hit. (Passive)
3. **Nature's Wrath:** Call down the roots of the earth to permanently entangle and crush a target, dealing 10d10 bludgeoning damage. (Cost: 1 AP, 60 MP)

### **Level 19 Abilities (Choose 1):**
1. **Feral Senses:** You can fight perfectly while blinded, and you always know the location of invisible creatures within 30ft. (Passive)
2. **Foe Slayer:** Add your Wisdom/Ingenuity modifier to the attack roll OR the damage roll of an attack you make. You can do this before or after the roll. (Passive)
3. **Silver Chariot:** Teleport up to 50ft away, leaving behind a 5ft circle that deals 1d20 damage per turn to enemies standing in it. *(Note: Flint's selected ability)* (Cost: 1 AP, 40 MP)

### **Level 2 Skills (Choose 1):**
1. **Fighting Style (Archery):** Gain +2 to Aim checks. Ranged attacks ignore half cover. (Passive)
2. **Fighting Style (Two-Weapon Fighting):** Add your ability modifier to the damage of your off-hand weapon attack. (Passive)
3. **Natural Explorer:** Your movement speed increases by +10ft, and you can move stealthily at a normal pace. (Passive)

### **Level 4 Skills (Choose 1):**
1. **Primeval Awareness:** By spending 1 minute in concentration, you can sense the presence of any aberrations, celestials, dragons, elementals, fey, fiends, or undead within 1 mile. (Once/day)
2. **Camouflage:** You can craft camouflage out of mud, dirt, and leaves, granting you +10 to Stealth checks. (Passive)
3. **Hide in Plain Sight:** You can attempt to hide even when you are only lightly obscured by foliage, heavy rain, or mist. (Passive)

### **Level 6 Skills (Choose 1):**
1. **Greater Favored Enemy:** Choose a second favored enemy type. The damage bonus increases to +4. (Passive)
2. **Vanish:** You can use the Hide action as a bonus action on your turn. (Passive)
3. **Poisoner's Kit:** You can coat your arrows or blades in a natural poison that deals +1d4 poison damage. (Passive)

### **Level 8 Skills (Choose 1):**
1. **Fleet of Foot:** You can take the Dash action as a bonus action. (Passive)
2. **Land's Stride:** Moving through nonmagical difficult terrain costs you no extra movement, and you take no damage from thorny plants. (Passive)
3. **Deadly Aim:** Before you make a ranged attack, you can choose to take a -5 penalty to the attack roll. If the attack hits, you add +10 to the damage. (Passive)

### **Level 10 Skills (Choose 1):**
1. **Tireless:** You can reduce your exhaustion level by 1 upon finishing a short rest. (Once/day)
2. **Nature's Veil:** You can draw on the magic of nature to turn yourself invisible until the start of your next turn. (Once/day)
3. **Sniper's Nest:** If you do not move on your turn, your ranged attacks have advantage. (Passive)

### **Level 12 Skills (Choose 1):**
1. **Iron Mind:** You gain proficiency in Will saving throws. (Passive)
2. **Cull the Herd:** Your attacks deal an extra 1d8 damage to enemies that are below 50% HP. (Passive)
3. **Wilderness Survival:** You are immune to extreme cold and extreme heat, and you can forage enough food to feed 10 people easily. (Passive)

### **Level 14 Skills (Choose 1):**
1. **Ghostly Stride:** You can move through solid objects and creatures as if they were difficult terrain for 1 minute. (Once/day)
2. **Blind Spot:** If an enemy misses you with a ranged attack, you can instantly become hidden to them. (Passive)
3. **Beast's Tenacity:** Gain resistance to poison and immunity to disease. (Passive)

### **Level 16 Skills (Choose 1):**
1. **Perfect Shot:** Once per day, you can declare an attack a "Perfect Shot." It automatically hits, ignores all cover, and deals maximum damage. (Once/day)
2. **Aura of the Hunt:** Allies within 30ft of you gain the benefits of your Favored Enemy and Favored Terrain features. (Passive)
3. **Relentless Tracker:** Once you have tracked a creature, you always know its general direction relative to you, even across planes. (Passive)

### **Level 18 Skills (Choose 1):**
1. **Master of the Wilds:** You can cast the *Commune with Nature* spell as a free action without expending MP. (Once/day)
2. **Unseen Attacker:** When you attack from hidden, your position is not revealed if the attack misses. (Passive)
3. **Ricochet:** If you miss with a ranged attack, the projectile bounces and attacks a secondary target within 10ft of the first. (Passive)

### **Level 20 Skills (Choose 1):**
1. **Avatar of the Hunt:** You become the ultimate predator. You are permanently invisible to your Favored Enemies, and you deal double damage to them. (Passive)
2. **Rain of Death:** You can fire a ranged weapon up to 100 times in a single turn, distributing the shots among any targets in range. (Once/week)
3. **Unyielding Survival:** You simply cannot die in your Favored Terrain. If reduced to 0 HP, you instantly teleport to a safe location in the terrain with full HP. (Passive)
