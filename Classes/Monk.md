_"Masters of martial arts, channeling their internal Ki to achieve superhuman feats."_
**Mana Dependency** : 2/5
## Class Focus (Single-Class Only)
_Granted at Character Creation. Lost if you multiclass. Choose ONE:_
1. **Flawless Form:** If you take the "Dodge" or "Defend" action, the first attack that hits you is completely negated, and the attacker takes the damage instead.
2. **Way of the Open Hand:** Your unarmed strikes bypass all physical resistances and your base unarmed damage becomes 1d10.
3. **Ki Master:** You regain 10 MP (or 1 Ki point) every time you critically hit an enemy or successfully dodge an attack.

### **Class Perk (Level 1): Martial Arts**
- Your unarmed strikes deal 1d4 + STR/AGI damage (increases to 1d6 at Lvl 5, 1d8 at Lvl 10, 1d10 at Lvl 15).
- Unarmored Defense: While wearing no armor, your base ARN is equal to `10 + Agility mod + Will mod`.
- You can use Agility instead of Strength for all unarmed and monk weapon attacks.

### **Bonus Perk (Level 10 - Single-Class Only): Perfect Soul**
- You gain immunity to poison, disease, and aging.
- Your unarmed strikes count as magical weapons with a +3 enhancement bonus.

--- 

### **Level 1 Abilities (Choose 1):**
1. **Flurry of Blows:** Immediately after taking the Attack action, make two unarmed strikes as a bonus. (Cost: 10 MP)
2. **Patient Defense:** Take the Dodge action as a bonus action, giving attacks against you disadvantage. (Cost: 10 MP)
3. **Step of the Wind:** Disengage or Dash as a bonus action, and your jump distance is doubled for the turn. (Cost: 10 MP)

### **Level 3 Abilities (Choose 1):**
1. **Deflect Missiles:** Catch a ranged projectile out of the air, reducing damage to 0, and immediately throw it back at the attacker. (Cost: 10 MP, Reaction)
2. **Open Hand Technique:** When you hit with Flurry of Blows, the target must make a STR save or be knocked prone. (Passive)
3. **Shadow Step:** Teleport up to 60ft from one shadow to another shadow. (Cost: 10 MP)

### **Level 5 Abilities (Choose 1):**
1. **Extra Attack:** Make two attacks when you take the Attack action. (Cost: 1 AP)
2. **Stunning Strike:** When you hit a creature with a melee weapon attack, force them to make a RES save or be stunned until the end of your next turn. (Cost: 15 MP)
3. **Fists of Fire:** Your unarmed strikes deal an additional 1d6 fire damage for 1 minute. (Cost: 10 MP)

### **Level 7 Abilities (Choose 1):**
1. **Evasion:** When subjected to an AoE effect that allows a DEX save for half damage, you take no damage on a success and half on a fail. (Passive)
2. **Stillness of Mind:** You can use an action to automatically end one effect on yourself that is causing you to be charmed or frightened. (Cost: 1 AP)
3. **Water Whip:** A whip of water strikes a target 30ft away. Deals 3d10 bludgeoning damage and pulls them 10ft closer. (Cost: 20 MP)

### **Level 9 Abilities (Choose 1):**
1. **Quivering Palm:** Strike a creature with invisible vibrations. You can choose to end their life instantly at any point in the next month (RES save drops them to 0 HP instead). (Cost: 30 MP)
2. **Wall of Wind:** Create a wall of strong wind that deflects all arrows and projectiles and deals damage to enemies passing through. (Cost: 20 MP)
3. **Sun Shield:** Emit a blinding aura. Any creature that hits you with a melee attack takes 2d8 radiant damage. (Cost: 15 MP)

### **Level 11 Abilities (Choose 1):**
1. **Diamond Soul:** You gain proficiency in ALL saving throws. If you fail a save, you can spend 10 MP to reroll it. (Passive)
2. **Cloak of Shadows:** Become completely invisible as long as you remain in dim light or darkness. (Cost: 10 MP)
3. **Fists of the Elements:** Your unarmed strikes deal +2d6 damage of your choice (Fire, Cold, Lightning, or Earth). (Cost: 20 MP)

### **Level 13 Abilities (Choose 1):**
1. **Tongue of the Sun and Moon:** You can understand all spoken languages, and any creature that can speak can understand you. (Passive)
2. **Breath of Winter:** Exhale a 30ft cone of freezing cold air dealing 6d10 cold damage and slowing enemies. (Cost: 25 MP)
3. **Astral Projection:** Project your spirit out of your body, allowing you to walk through walls, fly, and scout without physical harm. (Cost: 30 MP)

### **Level 15 Abilities (Choose 1):**
1. **Timeless Body:** You no longer need food or water, and you cannot be magically aged. (Passive)
2. **Touch of Death:** A precise strike to a nerve cluster deals 10d10 necrotic damage and permanently reduces the target's max HP by that amount. (Cost: 30 MP)
3. **Diamond Body:** You become immune to critical hits and sneak attacks. (Passive)

### **Level 17 Abilities (Choose 1):**
1. **Empty Body:** You become invisible for 1 minute and gain resistance to all damage except force damage. (Cost: 40 MP)
2. **One Finger Death Punch:** A legendary strike. If it hits, it deals 20d10 force damage. If the target survives, they are stunned for 3 turns. (Cost: 50 MP)
3. **Dragon Posture:** You emit an aura of draconic fear. All enemies within 30ft have disadvantage on attack rolls against you. (Cost: 20 MP)

### **Level 19 Abilities (Choose 1):**
1. **Perfect Self:** When you roll for initiative and have no MP left, you regain 40 MP automatically. (Passive)
2. **Thousand Fists:** Make an unarmed strike against every single enemy within 30ft of you in the blink of an eye. (Cost: 40 MP)
3. **Ascension:** You can fly at a speed of 100ft and no longer need to breathe. (Passive)

---

### **Level 2 Skills (Choose 1):**
1. **Unarmored Movement:** Your speed increases by +10ft while you are not wearing armor or wielding a shield. (Passive)
2. **Slow Fall:** You can use your reaction to reduce any falling damage you take to 0. (Passive)
3. **Acrobat:** You have advantage on all Agility and Reflex skill checks. (Passive)

### **Level 4 Skills (Choose 1):**
1. **Purity of Body:** You are immune to disease and poison. (Passive)
2. **Iron Fists:** Your unarmed strikes deal double damage to objects and structures (doors, walls, shields). (Passive)
3. **Zen Archery:** You can use your Monk weapons and Unarmed combat features with a Shortbow or Longbow. (Passive)

### **Level 6 Skills (Choose 1):**
1. **Ki-Empowered Strikes:** Your unarmed strikes count as magical for the purpose of overcoming resistances and immunities. (Passive)
2. **Healing Hands:** You can heal yourself or an ally for 2d10 HP by touching them. (Once/day)
3. **Feather Step:** You can run across liquids (like water or acid) and along vertical walls without falling. (Passive)

### **Level 8 Skills (Choose 1):**
1. **Evasion Mastery:** If an attack misses you, you can immediately make an unarmed strike against the attacker. (Passive)
2. **Mind over Matter:** Ignore all levels of exhaustion for 24 hours. (Once/day)
3. **Vibrating Strike:** Your unarmed strikes ignore target's ARN entirely. (Passive)

### **Level 10 Skills (Choose 1):**
1. **Tranquility:** You permanently benefit from a *Sanctuary* spell. Enemies must make a Will save to even attempt to attack you. (Passive)
2. **Inner Fire:** You are immune to extreme cold and extreme heat environments. (Passive)
3. **Ki Block:** When you hit a creature with an unarmed strike, they cannot cast spells until the end of their next turn. (Passive)

### **Level 12 Skills (Choose 1):**
1. **Diamond Mind:** You have advantage on all Will and Intelligence saving throws. (Passive)
2. **Whirlwind Step:** You can move through enemy spaces without provoking opportunity attacks. (Passive)
3. **Unyielding Stance:** You cannot be knocked prone, pushed, or grappled by any creature huge or smaller. (Passive)

### **Level 14 Skills (Choose 1):**
1. **Iron Shirt:** Gain physical damage resistance against slashing, piercing, and bludgeoning damage. (Passive)
2. **Sightless Eye:** You gain blindsight up to 30ft, allowing you to fight perfectly in magical darkness or while blinded. (Passive)
3. **Ki Transfusion:** You can transfer your own HP to an ally, or drain an enemy's HP to heal yourself on a hit. (Once/day)

### **Level 16 Skills (Choose 1):**
1. **Supreme Reflexes:** You can take two Reactions per round instead of one. (Passive)
2. **Shatter Point:** You can find the exact weak point of an enemy. Your next attack automatically critically hits. (Once/day)
3. **Aura of Peace:** Enemies within 30ft of you must make a Will save to take any hostile action (attacking, casting offensive spells). (Passive)

### **Level 18 Skills (Choose 1):**
1. **Eternal Youth:** Your body reverts to its prime age, and you gain +2 to Agility, Reflex, and Coordination. (Passive)
2. **Ghost Strike:** Your unarmed strikes can hit ethereal creatures and travel through solid walls to hit targets on the other side. (Passive)
3. **Absolute Focus:** For 1 minute, you cannot miss any attacks, and you automatically succeed all saving throws. (Once/day)

### **Level 20 Skills (Choose 1):**
1. **Avatar of Combat:** You become the ultimate martial artist. Your Unarmed damage becomes 2d12, and you gain 4 attacks per action. (Passive)
2. **Nirvana:** You transcend physical reality. You can turn incorporeal at will, passing through anything and ignoring all physical damage. (Passive)
3. **The 100-Fist Strike:** Stop time for 1 round. During this stopped time, you can make 100 unarmed strikes against a single target. (Once/week)
