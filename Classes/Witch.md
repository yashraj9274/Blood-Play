_"Mystics who wield curses, hexes, and blood magic."_
**Mana Dependency** : 5/5
## Class Focus (Single-Class Only)
_Granted at Character Creation. Lost if you multiclass. Choose ONE:_
1. **Hexweaver:** Enemies suffering from your curses, hexes, or blights cannot heal by any means (magical or natural) until the curse is lifted.
2. **Blood Magic:** You can use your own HP or a willing ally's HP instead of MP to cast spells (10 HP = 10 MP).
3. **Coven's Protection:** When you take damage, you can immediately teleport up to 30ft away as a reaction, leaving behind a decoy that explodes for 2d6 poison damage.

### **Class Perk (Level 1): Witchcraft & Familiars**
- You use Intelligence or Charisma (your choice) as your spellcasting modifier.
- You gain a magical familiar (cat, toad, raven) that can deliver touch spells for you.
- You excel at brewing potions and crafting magical talismans during short rests.

### **Bonus Perk (Level 10 - Single-Class Only): Mother of Curses**
- Your curses cannot be dispelled by normal magic (requires a *Wish* or *Miracle*).
- You can maintain concentration on two hexes simultaneously.

### **Level 1 Abilities (Choose 1):**
1. **Hex:** Curse a target. You deal an extra 1d6 necrotic damage to them whenever you hit them with an attack, and they have disadvantage on one stat's checks. (Cost: 0 AP, Bonus, 10 MP)
2. **Tasha's Hideous Laughter:** Target must make a Will save or fall prone laughing uncontrollably, unable to take actions. (Cost: 1 AP, 10 MP)
3. **Inflict Wounds:** Touch attack dealing a massive 3d10 necrotic damage. (Cost: 1 AP, 15 MP)

### **Level 3 Abilities (Choose 1):**
1. **Blindness/Deafness:** Target makes a RES save or goes completely blind or deaf for 1 minute. (Cost: 1 AP, 15 MP)
2. **Crown of Madness:** A jagged crown appears on the target's head. They are charmed and must use their action to attack their nearest ally. (Cost: 1 AP, 15 MP)
3. **Ray of Enfeeblement:** A black beam saps the target's strength. Their melee attacks deal half damage for 1 minute (RES save negates). (Cost: 1 AP, 15 MP)

### **Level 5 Abilities (Choose 1):**
1. **Bestow Curse:** Curse a target. They take an extra 1d8 necrotic damage from your attacks, and must make a Will save every turn or lose their action. (Cost: 1 AP, 25 MP)
2. **Vampiric Touch:** Touch attack dealing 3d6 necrotic damage. You heal HP equal to the damage dealt. (Cost: 1 AP, 20 MP)
3. **Fear:** Project a phantasmal image of the target's worst nightmare. Targets in a 30ft cone must drop their weapons and flee. (Cost: 1 AP, 25 MP)

### **Level 7 Abilities (Choose 1):**
1. **Blight:** Drain the moisture and vitality from a target, dealing 8d8 necrotic damage. (Cost: 1 AP, 35 MP)
2. **Polymorph:** Transform an enemy into a harmless toad or slug for 1 hour (Will save negates). (Cost: 1 AP, 40 MP)
3. **Phantasmal Killer:** Trap the target in an illusion of their greatest fear. They take 4d10 psychic damage every turn they remain frightened. (Cost: 1 AP, 35 MP)

### **Level 9 Abilities (Choose 1):**
1. **Contagion:** Afflict an enemy with a horrific disease that rots their flesh, causing them to be permanently stunned, blinded, or weakened. (Cost: 1 AP, 45 MP)
2. **Dominate Person:** Take complete control of a humanoid target's actions for 1 minute. (Cost: 1 AP, 40 MP)
3. **Geas:** Place a magical command on a creature to carry out a service or refrain from an action. If they disobey, they take 5d10 psychic damage each day. (Cost: 1 AP, 45 MP)

### **Level 11 Abilities (Choose 1):**
1. **Eyebite:** Afflict enemies with panic, sickness, or sleep just by looking at them each turn for 1 minute. (Cost: 1 AP, 50 MP)
2. **Flesh to Stone:** Turn a creature completely into solid stone permanently (RES save). (Cost: 1 AP, 55 MP)
3. **Harm:** Unleash a virulent disease dealing 14d6 necrotic damage and reducing the target's Max HP by the same amount. (Cost: 1 AP, 60 MP)

### **Level 13 Abilities (Choose 1):**
1. **Finger of Death:** Point at a creature. Deals 7d8 + 30 necrotic damage. If killed, they permanently become a zombie under your command. (Cost: 1 AP, 75 MP)
2. **Mirage Arcane:** Make a 1-mile radius of terrain look, sound, smell, and feel like some other sort of terrain completely. (Cost: 1 AP, 70 MP)
3. **Symbol (Pain/Death):** Scribe a hidden rune that triggers when approached, dealing 10d10 necrotic damage or paralyzing everything in 60ft. (Cost: 1 AP, 70 MP)

### **Level 15 Abilities (Choose 1):**
1. **Feeblemind:** Shatter a target's intellect. They can no longer cast spells, communicate, or use magic items (Will save negates). (Cost: 1 AP, 80 MP)
2. **Maddening Darkness:** Create a 60ft sphere of absolute magical darkness. Targets inside take 8d8 psychic damage per turn and may go insane. (Cost: 1 AP, 85 MP)
3. **Clone:** Grow an inert duplicate of yourself. If you die, your soul instantly transfers to the clone. (Cost: 100 MP, Ritual)

### **Level 17 Abilities (Choose 1):**
1. **Weird:** Plunge a 30ft area into a nightmare dimension. Targets take 4d10 psychic damage per turn and are paralyzed by fear. (Cost: 1 AP, 110 MP)
2. **Imprisonment:** Bind a creature permanently in a state of suspended animation, burying them or trapping them in a gem. (Cost: 2 AP, 130 MP)
3. **True Polymorph:** Permanently turn yourself, an ally, or an enemy into any creature or object of equal or lower CR. (Cost: 2 AP, 150 MP)

### **Level 19 Abilities (Choose 1):**
1. **Power Word Kill:** Utter a single word of death. If the target has less than 100 HP, they die instantly. (Cost: 1 AP, 120 MP)
2. **Coven's Wrath:** Call down a plague of frogs, blood rain, and darkness over a 5-mile radius, dealing 10d10 necrotic/acid damage to all enemies. (Cost: 2 AP, 150 MP)
3. **Grand Hex:** You completely erase a target's ability to heal, use magic, or deal damage permanently. (Cost: 2 AP, 130 MP)

### **Level 2 Skills (Choose 1):**
1. **Brewmaster:** You have advantage on all crafting checks for potions and poisons. (Passive)
2. **Evil Eye:** As a reaction, you can impose disadvantage on an enemy's attack roll against you just by glaring at them. (Once/day)
3. **Familiar's Sight:** You can see through your familiar's eyes and hear through its ears at any distance. (Passive)

### **Level 4 Skills (Choose 1):**
1. **Aura of Misfortune:** Enemies within 10ft of you subtract 1d4 from all their attack rolls and saving throws. (Passive)
2. **Blood Drinker:** When you deal necrotic damage, you heal for 25% of the damage dealt. (Passive)
3. **Talisman of Ward:** You can create a small trinket that grants the wearer +2 to all saving throws. (Passive)

### **Level 6 Skills (Choose 1):**
1. **Voodoo Doll:** You can bind a doll to a creature. Any damage you deal to the doll is transferred directly to the creature, ignoring armor and distance. (Once/day)
2. **Cackling Charm:** Your laughter is infectious. Enemies who hear it must make a Will save or be charmed by you. (Once/day)
3. **Flight of the Night:** You can enchant a broom, staff, or mortar to fly at a speed of 60ft. (Passive)

### **Level 8 Skills (Choose 1):**
1. **Hag's Resilience:** You gain resistance to bludgeoning, piercing, and slashing damage from non-magical weapons. (Passive)
2. **Hexing Strike:** When you hit a creature with a weapon attack, you can apply your *Hex* spell as a free action. (Passive)
3. **Mind Shield:** Your thoughts cannot be read, and you are immune to being frightened. (Passive)

### **Level 10 Skills (Choose 1):**
1. **Curse of the Toad:** When an enemy critically hits you, you can use your reaction to turn them into a toad until the end of their next turn. (Once/day)
2. **Iron Will:** You have advantage on all Will and Intelligence saving throws. (Passive)
3. **Apothecary's Secret:** Your healing potions heal maximum HP, and your poisons deal double damage. (Passive)

### **Level 12 Skills (Choose 1):**
1. **Life Siphon Aura:** Enemies within 15ft of you take 1d6 necrotic damage at the start of their turns, and you heal for the total amount. (Passive Aura)
2. **Shadow Step:** You can teleport between shadows up to 60ft away as a free action. (Once/day)
3. **Speak with Dead:** You can permanently communicate with spirits and corpses without expending MP. (Passive)

### **Level 14 Skills (Choose 1):**
1. **Unbreakable Hex:** Your curses and hexes no longer require concentration to maintain. (Passive)
2. **Blood Puppet:** You can take control of a creature's physical body by manipulating their blood (Will save negates). (Once/day)
3. **Aura of Rot:** Non-magical weapons that strike you rust or rot instantly, dealing half damage and breaking. (Passive)

### **Level 16 Skills (Choose 1):**
1. **Coven's Strength:** For every ally within 30ft, your spells deal an extra +1d6 damage. (Passive)
2. **Master of Illusions:** Your illusion spells become physical reality for up to 1 minute. (Once/day)
3. **Grave Touched:** You no longer need to breathe, eat, or sleep, and you stop aging. (Passive)

### **Level 18 Skills (Choose 1):**
1. **Immortal Hag:** If you are killed, you resurrect 24 hours later in a new body formed from the nearest swamp or forest. (Passive)
2. **Perfect Curse:** The target of your *Bestow Curse* has disadvantage on ALL rolls, takes double damage from ALL sources, and cannot heal. (Once/day)
3. **Soul Weaver:** You can directly pull the soul out of an enemy, killing them instantly if they fail an extreme Will save. (Once/day)

### **Level 20 Skills (Choose 1):**
1. **Mother of Monsters:** You can permanently mutate an ally or yourself, granting them massive wings, claws, or an extra head (doubling actions). (Once/month)
2. **Avatar of the Coven:** You channel the power of an entire coven. For 1 minute, you can cast two spells per turn for 0 MP. (Once/week)
3. **The Final Hex:** You speak a curse so powerful it permanently erases the target from existence. Not even a Wish spell can bring them back. (Once/lifetime)
