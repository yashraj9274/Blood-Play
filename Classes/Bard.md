_"Artists who use magic through performance, charm, and illusion."_
**Mana Dependency** : 4/5
## Class Focus (Single-Class Only)
_Granted at Character Creation. Lost if you multiclass. Choose ONE:_
1. **Encore:** Once per combat, you can grant an ally a free, immediate action that interrupts the current turn order.
2. **Silver Tongue:** You can use your Charisma (Deception/Seduction/Ingenuity) instead of Aim or Strength for weapon attacks.
3. **Maestro's Aura:** Allies within 30ft of you naturally regenerate 1d4 MP per turn as long as you are conscious.

### **Class Perk (Level 1): Bardic Inspiration**
- Grant a d6 inspiration die to an ally (adds to attack, damage, or check).
- Die increases at Class Levels 5 (d8), 10 (d10), 15 (d12).
- Number of uses = Your Ingenuity modifier per long rest.

### **Bonus Perk (Level 10 - Single-Class Only): Magical Secrets**
- Learn 2 spells from any class's spell list.
- Your Bardic Inspiration die becomes d12.
- You can use Bardic Inspiration on yourself.

### **Level 1 Abilities (Choose 1):**
1. **Vicious Mockery:** Insult an enemy. They must make a Will save or take 1d4 psychic damage and have disadvantage on their next attack. (Cost: 1 AP)
2. **Healing Word:** Shout a word of restoration, healing an ally for 1d4 + Ingenuity from up to 60ft away. (Cost: 1 AP)
3. **Thunderwave:** A pulse of sound pushes all enemies in a 15ft cube away and deals 2d8 thunder damage. (Cost: 1 AP)

### **Level 3 Abilities (Choose 1):**
1. **Cutting Words:** Use a reaction to subtract your Bardic Inspiration die from an enemy's attack roll or damage. (Cost: 1 AP, Reaction)
2. **Dissonant Whispers:** Target must make a Will save or take 3d6 psychic damage and use its reaction to run away from you. (Cost: 1 AP)
3. **Shatter:** A loud ringing noise deals 3d8 thunder damage in a 10ft radius. (Cost: 1 AP)

### **Level 5 Abilities (Choose 1):**
1. **Hypnotic Pattern:** Create a swirling illusion. Enemies in a 30ft cube must make a Will save or be paralyzed (charmed) for 1 minute. (Cost: 1 AP)
2. **Font of Inspiration:** You regain all your Bardic Inspiration uses on a short rest instead of a long rest. (Passive)
3. **Mantle of Majesty:** You can cast the *Command* spell as a bonus action without expending MP. (Cost: 0 AP)

### **Level 7 Abilities (Choose 1):**
1. **Confusion:** Warp the minds of enemies in a 10ft radius. They roll a d10 to determine their random actions each turn. (Cost: 1 AP)
2. **Greater Invisibility:** Turn yourself or an ally invisible for 1 minute. The invisibility does not break when attacking. (Cost: 1 AP)
3. **Charm Monster:** Make any creature (not just humanoids) friendly toward you for 1 hour. (Cost: 1 AP)

### **Level 9 Abilities (Choose 1):**
1. **Synaptic Static:** Deal 8d6 psychic damage in a 20ft radius. Targets failing a Will save subtract 1d6 from all attacks for 1 minute. (Cost: 1 AP)
2. **Dominate Person:** Take complete control of a humanoid target's actions for 1 minute. (Cost: 1 AP)
3. **Mass Cure Wounds:** Heal up to 6 allies in a 30ft radius for 3d8 + Ingenuity. (Cost: 1 AP)

### **Level 11 Abilities (Choose 1):**
1. **Otto's Irresistible Dance:** Target is forced to dance wildly, granting allies advantage to hit them and taking away their movement. (Cost: 1 AP)
2. **Eyebite:** Afflict enemies with panic, sickness, or sleep just by looking at them. (Cost: 1 AP)
3. **Programmed Illusion:** Create a massive, complex illusion with sound and smell that follows a script you design. (Cost: 1 AP)

### **Level 13 Abilities (Choose 1):**
1. **Forcecage:** Trap a creature in an invisible, unbreakable box of force magic for 1 hour. (Cost: 1 AP)
2. **Resurrection:** Bring a dead ally back to life with full HP. (Cost: 2 AP)
3. **Prismatic Spray:** Fire a blinding ray of rainbow light dealing massive random elemental damage types. (Cost: 1 AP)

### **Level 15 Abilities (Choose 1):**
1. **Glibness:** Your magic makes lying flawless. Any Deception or Seduction check you make is automatically a natural 20. (Passive)
2. **Mind Blank:** Grant an ally total immunity to psychic damage and mind-reading/charm effects for 24 hours. (Cost: 1 AP)
3. **Feeblemind:** Shatter a target's intellect. They can no longer cast spells or communicate (Will save negates). (Cost: 1 AP)

### **Level 17 Abilities (Choose 1):**
1. **Power Word Kill:** Utter a single word. If the target has less than 100 HP, they die instantly. (Cost: 1 AP)
2. **Foresight:** Grant an ally advantage on ALL rolls, and impose disadvantage on ALL attacks against them for 8 hours. (Cost: 1 AP)
3. **Mass Polymorph:** Turn up to 10 enemies into harmless turtles, or up to 10 allies into T-Rexes. (Cost: 1 AP)

### **Level 19 Abilities (Choose 1):**
1. **True Polymorph:** Permanently turn any creature or object into another creature or object. (Cost: 2 AP)
2. **Wish:** Cast the most powerful spell in reality. Alter the fabric of the universe to your whim. (Cost: 2 AP)
3. **Chorus of the Gods:** All allies on the battlefield receive a Bardic Inspiration die every single turn automatically. (Passive)

### **Level 2 Skills (Choose 1):**
1. **Jack of All Trades:** Add half your proficiency bonus to any skill check you aren't proficient in. (Passive)
2. **Song of Rest:** Allies heal an extra 1d6 HP during a short rest. (Passive)
3. **Fascinate:** Play music to draw the attention of everyone in a room, making them ignore your allies' stealthy actions. (Once/day)

### **Level 4 Skills (Choose 1):**
1. **Expertise:** Choose two skills. Your proficiency bonus is doubled for them. (Passive)
2. **Actor:** You can flawlessly mimic the voice and speech patterns of anyone you've heard speak for 1 minute. (Passive)
3. **Distracting Flourish:** You put on a display that forces all enemies to look at you, allowing an ally to escape. (Once/day)

### **Level 6 Skills (Choose 1):**
1. **Countercharm:** Play a tune that gives all allies advantage on saves against being frightened or charmed. (Passive Aura)
2. **Additional Magical Secrets:** Learn 2 more spells from any class. (Passive)
3. **Silver Tongue:** You can never roll below a 10 on a Deception or Seduction check. (Passive)

### **Level 8 Skills (Choose 1):**
1. **Battle Magic:** After casting a spell, you can make a weapon attack as a free action. (Passive)
2. **Inspiring Leader:** Give a 10-minute speech that grants 6 allies Temporary AHP equal to your Level + Ingenuity. (Once/day)
3. **Unfailing Inspiration:** If an ally rolls your Inspiration die and fails the check, they keep the die. (Passive)

### **Level 10 Skills (Choose 1):**
1. **Peerless Skill:** You can use your own Bardic Inspiration die to boost your own skill checks. (Passive)
2. **Aura of Majesty:** Enemies must make a Will save to attack you. If they fail, they must choose another target. (Passive)
3. **Words of Terror:** Speak privately to someone for 1 minute. They become irrationally terrified of a target of your choice. (Once/day)

### **Level 12 Skills (Choose 1):**
1. **Magical Ambush:** If you cast a spell while hidden, the targets have disadvantage on their saving throws. (Passive)
2. **Ventriloquism:** You can cast spells originating from any point within 60ft, keeping your position hidden. (Passive)
3. **Universal Speech:** You can speak, read, and understand all languages. (Passive)

### **Level 14 Skills (Choose 1):**
1. **Infectious Inspiration:** When an ally uses your Inspiration die, you can immediately give another one to a different ally for free. (Passive)
2. **Master of Illusions:** Your illusions become physical reality for up to 1 minute. (Once/day)
3. **Beguiling Magic:** When you cast an Enchantment or Illusion spell, a creature of your choice is automatically charmed by you. (Passive)

### **Level 16 Skills (Choose 1):**
1. **Superior Inspiration:** If you roll initiative and have no Bardic Inspiration uses left, you regain one. (Passive)
2. **Dirge of Doom:** Enemies within 30ft of you cannot benefit from healing or temporary HP. (Passive Aura)
3. **Virtuoso:** Your Bardic Inspiration die becomes a d20. (Passive)

### **Level 18 Skills (Choose 1):**
1. **Unbreakable Charm:** Your charm effects can no longer be broken by the target taking damage. (Passive)
2. **Symphony of Destruction:** Your thunder and psychic damage rolls are maximized automatically. (Passive)
3. **Enthralling Performance:** Give a performance that completely mind-controls an entire crowd of up to 100 people for 1 hour. (Once/day)

### **Level 20 Skills (Choose 1):**
1. **Avatar of the Muses:** You embody the arts. You gain +4 to Ingenuity and Deception, and your spells cost 0 MP. (Once/day for 1 minute)
2. **Rewrite Reality:** You can change one event that happened in the last 24 hours as if it was a bad script. (Once/week)
3. **Eternal Fame:** You are recognized everywhere you go. Factions will give you free items, housing, and unquestioning loyalty. (Passive)
