_"Guardians of the wild, wielding nature's wrath and the power of shapeshifting."_
**Mana Dependency** : 3/5
## Class Focus (Single-Class Only)
_Granted at Character Creation. Lost if you multiclass. Choose ONE:_
1. **Nature's Wrath:** While in a wild shape, your attacks deal an additional 1d10 elemental damage corresponding to your current environment (fire in desert, cold in snow, poison in swamp, etc).
2. **Circle of Life:** Whenever you cast a spell, you and all allies within 10ft naturally heal 1d4 HP.
3. **Master Shapeshifter:** You can Wild Shape as a free action (0 AP) and your beast forms gain your base ARN.

### **Class Perk (Level 1): Wild Shape & Nature Magic**
- You can transform into a beast you have seen before. Your HP becomes the beast's HP; if it drops to 0, you revert to your normal form with your previous HP.
- You use Intelligence for your nature-based spellcasting (MP).
- You are proficient in light armor, medium armor (non-metal only), shields (non-metal), and simple weapons.

### **Bonus Perk (Level 10 - Single-Class Only): Archdruid**
- You can stay in Wild Shape indefinitely.
- You can cast spells while in Wild Shape.

--- 

### **Level 1 Abilities (Choose 1):**
1. **Wild Shape:** Transform into a beast of Challenge Rating 1 or lower (e.g., Wolf, Panther, Spider). (Cost: 1 AP)
2. **Entangle:** Weeds and vines wrap around enemies in a 20ft square, rooting them in place (STR save negates). (Cost: 1 AP, 10 MP)
3. **Thorn Whip:** A magical vine strikes out 30ft, dealing 1d6 piercing damage and pulling the target 10ft closer. (Cost: 1 AP, 5 MP)

### **Level 3 Abilities (Choose 1):**
1. **Moonbeam:** A silvery beam of pale light deals 2d10 radiant damage in a 5ft radius. Shapechangers have disadvantage. (Cost: 1 AP, 15 MP)
2. **Spike Growth:** The ground in a 20ft radius becomes covered in sharp thorns. Moving through it deals 2d4 piercing damage for every 5ft. (Cost: 1 AP, 15 MP)
3. **Flaming Sphere:** Summon a 5ft sphere of fire that you can ram into enemies for 2d6 fire damage. (Cost: 1 AP, 15 MP)

### **Level 5 Abilities (Choose 1):**
1. **Call Lightning:** A storm cloud strikes a target with a bolt of lightning for 3d10 damage. (Cost: 1 AP, 25 MP)
2. **Plant Growth:** Cause massive, thick vegetation to grow instantly, making a 100ft radius extremely difficult terrain. (Cost: 1 AP, 20 MP)
3. **Conjure Animals:** Summon fey spirits that take the form of beasts to fight for you. (Cost: 1 AP, 30 MP)

### **Level 7 Abilities (Choose 1):**
1. **Polymorph:** Transform a creature into a harmless beast (like a frog or sheep) for 1 hour (Will save negates). (Cost: 1 AP, 40 MP)
2. **Wall of Fire:** Create a blazing wall of fire that deals 5d8 fire damage to anyone passing through it. (Cost: 1 AP, 35 MP)
3. **Blight:** Drain the moisture and vitality from a target, dealing 8d8 necrotic damage. Plants take maximum damage automatically. (Cost: 1 AP, 35 MP)

### **Level 9 Abilities (Choose 1):**
1. **Tree Stride:** Step into a tree and instantly teleport to any other tree of the same kind within 500ft. (Cost: 1 AP, 30 MP)
2. **Awaken:** Touch a huge tree or animal and give it human-like sentience, allowing it to speak and fight for you permanently. (Cost: 2 AP, 100 MP)
3. **Insect Plague:** A massive 20ft radius swarm of locusts deals 4d10 piercing damage and heavily obscures the area. (Cost: 1 AP, 45 MP)

### **Level 11 Abilities (Choose 1):**
1. **Sunbeam:** Fire a blinding beam of light 60ft long. Deals 6d8 radiant damage and blinds enemies (RES save). (Cost: 1 AP, 55 MP)
2. **Wall of Thorns:** Create a massive wall of razor-sharp brush that deals 7d8 piercing damage to anyone attempting to push through. (Cost: 1 AP, 50 MP)
3. **Transport via Plants:** Create a magical link between two plants on the same plane of existence, allowing instant teleportation across the continent. (Cost: 1 AP, 60 MP)

### **Level 13 Abilities (Choose 1):**
1. **Fire Storm:** Create a massive storm of holy fire dealing 7d10 fire damage in a 100ft area. Plant life is unaffected. (Cost: 1 AP, 70 MP)
2. **Whirlwind:** Summon a massive tornado that sweeps up enemies, dealing 10d6 bludgeoning damage and throwing them 50ft away. (Cost: 1 AP, 70 MP)
3. **Reverse Gravity:** Reverse gravity in a 50ft radius cylinder, sending enemies falling upwards 100ft into the sky. (Cost: 1 AP, 75 MP)

### **Level 15 Abilities (Choose 1):**
1. **Tsunami:** Summon a massive wall of water 300ft long and 50ft high that crashes forward, crushing everything for 6d10 bludgeoning damage. (Cost: 2 AP, 90 MP)
2. **Animal Shapes:** Transform your entire party into huge beasts (like Mammoths or T-Rexes) for up to 24 hours. (Cost: 1 AP, 80 MP)
3. **Control Weather:** You take complete control of the weather in a 5-mile radius, summoning blizzards, hurricanes, or scorching heat. (Cost: 1 AP, 80 MP)

### **Level 17 Abilities (Choose 1):**
1. **Shapechange:** Transform into a Dragon, Beholder, or other powerful magical entity for 1 hour, gaining all their abilities. (Cost: 1 AP, 120 MP)
2. **Storm of Vengeance:** Summon a gargantuan acidic, lightning-filled storm that wreaks havoc over a 360ft radius for 10 turns. (Cost: 2 AP, 150 MP)
3. **Foresight:** Touch a willing creature. They gain advantage on ALL rolls, and disadvantage on ALL attacks against them for 8 hours. (Cost: 1 AP, 100 MP)

### **Level 19 Abilities (Choose 1):**
1. **Wrath of the Earth:** Cause a volcano to erupt beneath a target, or a chasm to swallow them whole, dealing 20d10 fire/bludgeoning damage. (Cost: 2 AP, 150 MP)
2. **World Tree Link:** You merge with the roots of the world, gaining infinite MP and knowing everything that happens across the continent for 10 minutes. (Cost: 1 AP)
3. **Elemental Monolith:** Summon an Elemental Lord (Fire, Earth, Water, Air) that fights for you. It has 1,000 HP and massive AoE attacks. (Cost: 2 AP, 150 MP)

### **Level 2 Skills (Choose 1):**
1. **Speak with Animals:** You can permanently communicate with all beasts natively. (Passive)
2. **Land's Stride:** Moving through non-magical difficult terrain costs you no extra movement, and you take no damage from natural hazards like thorns. (Passive)
3. **Beast Companion:** Gain a small, non-combat pet (like a raven or squirrel) that acts as a scout and spy. (Passive)

### **Level 4 Skills (Choose 1):**
1. **Primal Strike:** Your attacks in Wild Shape count as magical for the purpose of overcoming resistances. (Passive)
2. **Natural Recovery:** Regain half your Max MP when you take a short rest. (Once/day)
3. **Barkskin:** Your skin becomes tough as oak. Your base ARN can never be lower than 16. (Passive)

### **Level 6 Skills (Choose 1):**
1. **Improved Wild Shape:** You can now transform into flying and swimming beasts. (Passive)
2. **Circle Forms:** The maximum CR of beasts you can transform into increases significantly. (Passive)
3. **Venom Resistance:** You have resistance to poison damage and advantage on saves against being poisoned. (Passive)

### **Level 8 Skills (Choose 1):**
1. **Beast Speech:** You can cast spells with verbal components even while in animal form. (Passive)
2. **Elemental Attunement:** Choose an element (Fire, Cold, Lightning). You gain resistance to it. (Passive)
3. **Nature's Ward:** You cannot be charmed or frightened by fey or elementals. (Passive)

### **Level 10 Skills (Choose 1):**
1. **Thousand Forms:** You can cast the *Alter Self* spell at will without expending MP. (Passive)
2. **Verdant Growth:** Any barren land you walk across blooms with grass and flowers instantly. (Passive)
3. **Fey Step:** You can teleport up to 30ft to an unoccupied space you can see as a free action. (Once/day)

### **Level 12 Skills (Choose 1):**
1. **Timeless Body:** You age only 1 year for every 10 years that pass, and you cannot be magically aged. (Passive)
2. **Moon Sickle:** Your spells that heal restore an extra 1d4 HP per spell level. (Passive)
3. **Apex Form:** Your Wild Shapes gain +20 Max HP and +2 to their attack rolls. (Passive)

### **Level 14 Skills (Choose 1):**
1. **Nature's Sanctuary:** When a beast or plant creature attacks you, it must make a Will save or choose a different target. (Passive)
2. **Elemental Body:** When you are hit by a melee attack, the attacker takes 1d10 damage of your attuned element. (Passive)
3. **Feral Senses:** You don't have disadvantage when attacking invisible creatures, and you can sense them within 30ft. (Passive)

### **Level 16 Skills (Choose 1):**
1. **Beast Spells:** You can cast your Druid spells while in Wild Shape. (Passive)
2. **Aura of the Wild:** Allies within 30ft of you gain resistance to poison and elemental damage. (Passive)
3. **Regrowth:** If a limb is severed, it naturally regrows over the course of a long rest. (Passive)

### **Level 18 Skills (Choose 1):**
1. **Archdruid's Vigor:** You can use your Wild Shape ability an unlimited number of times. (Passive)
2. **Storm Lord:** You are completely immune to lightning and thunder damage. (Passive)
3. **Earth Glide:** You can move effortlessly through solid earth and stone as if it were liquid. (Passive)

### **Level 20 Skills (Choose 1):**
1. **Avatar of Nature:** You become the physical embodiment of nature. Your INT and RES increase by +4. (Passive)
2. **Reincarnate:** If you die, a new body grows from the earth 24 hours later, and your soul is transferred into it. (Passive)
3. **Wild Magic Surge:** Whenever you cast a spell, roll a d20. On a 20, the spell costs 0 MP and casts twice automatically. (Passive)
