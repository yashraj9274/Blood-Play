_"Masters of chemical reactions, potions, and mutations."_
**Mana Dependency** : 2/5
## Class Focus (Single-Class Only)
_Granted at Character Creation. Lost if you multiclass. Choose ONE:_
1. **Philosopher's Stone:** You can convert your own HP directly into MP, and MP directly into HP, as a free action without limits.
2. **Volatile Mixture:** All potions you throw deal bonus AoE splash damage equal to your Intelligence Modifier x 2.
3. **Elixir Mastery:** Any healing or buff potion you consume lasts twice as long and always heals the maximum possible HP.

### **Class Perk (Level 1): Chemical Crafting**
- You can craft basic potions (healing, acid, fire) during short rests without needing a lab.
- You gain proficiency with all throwables and blowdarts.
- Intelligence is your primary stat for attack and damage with alchemical items.

### **Bonus Perk (Level 10 - Single-Class Only): Master Mutagen**
- You can permanently alter your body, gaining +4 to a Core Stat of your choice.
- You become immune to poisons and diseases.

### **Level 1 Abilities (Choose 1):**
1. **Acid Flask:** Throw a vial of acid. Deals 1d8 + INT acid damage and reduces target's ARN by 2. (Cost: 1 AP)
2. **Healing Draught:** Toss or administer a potion healing 1d10 + Class Level HP. (Cost: 1 AP)
3. **Smoke Bomb:** Create a 15ft cloud of thick smoke. Grants heavy cover and blocks line of sight. (Cost: 1 AP)

### **Level 3 Abilities (Choose 1):**
1. **Adrenaline Shot:** Target ally gains an extra action on their next turn. (Cost: 1 AP)
2. **Sticky Fire:** Throw a flask that deals 1d6 fire damage initially, and 1d6 fire damage at the start of the target's turn for 3 turns. (Cost: 1 AP)
3. **Noxious Gas:** 15ft AoE. Enemies must make a RES save or be poisoned, taking 1d4 damage per turn. (Cost: 1 AP)

### **Level 5 Abilities (Choose 1):**
1. **Double Dose:** Throw two potions or flasks simultaneously. (Cost: 1 AP)
2. **Flashbang:** Enemies in a 20ft radius must make an INT save or be blinded for 1 turn. (Cost: 1 AP)
3. **Mutagenic Strike:** Your next melee attack injects a mutagen, causing the target to grow heavy, halving their movement speed. (Cost: 1 AP)

### **Level 7 Abilities (Choose 1):**
1. **Corrosive Blast:** A massive acid explosion dealing 3d8 acid damage and permanently shredding 5 ARN from targets hit. (Cost: 1 AP)
2. **Panacea:** Remove all negative conditions, curses, and poisons from a target. (Cost: 1 AP)
3. **Frost Vial:** Deals 2d6 cold damage and roots the target in place for 1 turn (RES save negates). (Cost: 1 AP)

### **Level 9 Abilities (Choose 1):**
1. **Berserker Brew:** Target ally gains +10 Strength and Resistance, but takes 1d6 damage every turn. Lasts 3 turns. (Cost: 1 AP)
2. **Alchemical Golem:** Summon an oozing construct (HP = your Max MP) to fight for you for 1 minute. (Cost: 1 AP)
3. **Transmutation Field:** Turn the ground in a 30ft radius into quicksand or spikes, severely altering the battlefield. (Cost: 1 AP)

### **Level 11 Abilities (Choose 1):**
1. **Elixir of Life:** Revive a fallen ally who died within the last 3 turns with 1 HP. (Cost: 1 AP)
2. **Explosive Chain:** Throw a bomb that bounces to two additional targets within 10ft of the first. (Cost: 1 AP)
3. **Mind-Muddle Potion:** Target must make an INT save or attack their nearest ally on their next turn. (Cost: 1 AP)

### **Level 13 Abilities (Choose 1):**
1. **Catalyst Surge:** Consume a potion that resets all your cooldowns instantly. (Cost: 1 AP)
2. **Petrification Vial:** Target must make a RES save or be turned to stone for 2 turns. (Cost: 1 AP)
3. **Aura of Decay:** For 3 turns, enemies within 10ft of you lose 1d10 HP and 1d10 MP at the start of their turns. (Cost: 1 AP)

### **Level 15 Abilities (Choose 1):**
1. **Homunculus Swarm:** Release tiny flying constructs that deal 5d6 piercing damage across a 30ft cone. (Cost: 1 AP)
2. **Liquid Courage:** You and all allies within 20ft become immune to fear, charm, and mind control for 10 minutes. (Cost: 1 AP)
3. **Absolute Solvent:** A powerful acid that completely destroys any non-magical weapon or armor it touches. (Cost: 1 AP)

### **Level 17 Abilities (Choose 1):**
1. **Tear Reality:** Throw a vial of pure void essence. Deals 10d10 force damage to a single target. (Cost: 1 AP)
2. **Fountain of Youth:** Heal all allies in a 30ft radius to full HP. (Cost: 1 AP)
3. **Alchemical Clone:** Create a perfect duplicate of yourself that has half your HP but can throw your potions. (Cost: 1 AP)

### **Level 19 Abilities (Choose 1):**
1. **The Philosopher's Bomb:** A localized nuke. Deals 20d12 fire/force damage in a 50ft radius. (Cost: 2 AP)
2. **Godhood Elixir:** For 3 turns, you are immune to all damage, and your spells/potions cost 0 MP. (Cost: 1 AP)
3. **Mass Transmutation:** Turn up to 5 enemies into harmless animals (frogs, sheep) for 1 minute. (RES save negates). (Cost: 1 AP)

### **Level 2 Skills (Choose 1):**
1. **Steady Hands:** You cannot accidentally drop items, and you gain advantage on all crafting checks. (Passive)
2. **Poisoner's Touch:** You can coat a weapon in poison as a free action. Next hit deals +1d6 poison damage. (Once/day)
3. **Swift Drinker:** You can drink a potion as a free action instead of an action. (Passive)

### **Level 4 Skills (Choose 1):**
1. **Iron Stomach:** You are completely immune to ingested poisons and bad food. (Passive)
2. **Experimental Vigor:** Your Max HP increases by +Class Level x 5. (Passive)
3. **Identify Compound:** You instantly know the exact properties and ingredients of any potion or chemical you see. (Passive)

### **Level 6 Skills (Choose 1):**
1. **Splash Zone:** Your single-target potions now affect adjacent targets for half damage/healing. (Passive)
2. **Mad Bomber:** Your explosive radius increases by +10ft. (Passive)
3. **Brewmaster:** You yield double the amount of potions when crafting. (Passive)

### **Level 8 Skills (Choose 1):**
1. **Chemical Coating:** Give an ally's weapon +2d6 acid damage for 1 hour. (Once/day)
2. **Mutant Agility:** Gain +2 to Agility and Reflex sub-stats permanently. (Passive)
3. **Volatile Blood:** When you take melee damage, your blood splashes the attacker for 1d4 acid damage. (Passive)

### **Level 10 Skills (Choose 1):**
1. **Master Alchemist:** All your potions roll their maximum possible healing/damage dice automatically. (Once/day for 1 minute)
2. **Bottled Elements:** You can change the damage type of any of your bombs (Fire, Cold, Lightning, Acid) at will. (Passive)
3. **Delayed Fuse:** You can set a bomb to detonate exactly when you want it to, up to 1 hour later. (Passive)

### **Level 12 Skills (Choose 1):**
1. **Venomous Veins:** You are healed by Poison and Acid damage instead of harmed by it. (Passive)
2. **Quick Toss:** You can throw a potion as a Reaction when an enemy moves. (Passive)
3. **Chameleon Skin:** You can blend into your surroundings, gaining +10 to Stealth checks. (Once/day, lasts 1 hour)

### **Level 14 Skills (Choose 1):**
1. **Liquid Anatomy:** Critical hits against you count as normal hits, as your organs shift around. (Passive)
2. **Elixir of Giant Strength:** Gain 24 Strength for 10 minutes. (Once/day)
3. **Endless Satchel:** You pull a random, highly potent potion out of your bag. Roll a d6 to determine the effect. (Once/day)

### **Level 16 Skills (Choose 1):**
1. **Toxophilite:** Enemies rolling saving throws against your potions roll with disadvantage. (Passive)
2. **Breath of the Dragon:** Exhale a 30ft cone of elemental damage of your choice (8d8). (Once/day)
3. **Regenerative Serum:** You naturally regenerate 5% of your Max HP at the start of your turn. (Passive)

### **Level 18 Skills (Choose 1):**
1. **Alchemical Ascension:** You no longer age, and you cannot die of old age or disease. (Passive)
2. **Aura of Protection:** Allies within 15ft take 20% less magical damage. (Passive)
3. **Omni-Cure:** You can cure any affliction, revive the recently dead, and fully restore a target with a single touch. (Once/day)

### **Level 20 Skills (Choose 1):**
1. **The Grand Experiment:** You temporarily break the laws of physics, gaining unlimited AP for 1 round. (Once/day)
2. **Avatar of Alchemy:** You become a being of pure elemental energy, gaining immunity to all non-magical attacks. (Once/day for 1 minute)
3. **Creation Spark:** You can permanently create any non-magical object out of thin air. (Once/day)
