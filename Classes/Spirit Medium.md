_"Conduits to the afterlife, manipulating souls, ghosts, and the ethereal plane."_
**Mana Dependency** : 4/5
## Class Focus (Single-Class Only)
_Granted at Character Creation. Lost if you multiclass. Choose ONE:_
1. **Ethereal Shift:** Phase into the spirit realm at will as a free action (0 AP). While phased, you are immune to physical damage, but cannot deal physical damage.
2. **Spirit Guide:** A permanent ghostly companion fights alongside you, acting independently to defend you or attack your foes.
3. **Soul Weaver:** Your spells and attacks can directly target an enemy's MP pool instead of their HP, draining their magic to fuel your own.

### **Class Perk (Level 1): Mediumship**
- You use Willpower as your spellcasting modifier.
- You can see, hear, and speak with ghosts and spirits natively.
- You can cast spells from the Spirit Medium list using MP.

### **Bonus Perk (Level 10 - Single-Class Only): Master of Souls**
- You can resurrect a dead creature without needing a physical body, binding their soul to an object (like a suit of armor or a doll).
- You are immune to necrotic and psychic damage.

--- 

### **Level 1 Abilities (Choose 1):**
1. **Soul Bolt:** Fire a blast of ethereal energy. Deals 1d10 force damage and ignores physical armor. (Cost: 1 AP, 5 MP)
2. **Spirit Shield:** Call a ghost to intercept an attack, granting yourself or an ally +5 AC for 1 turn. (Cost: 1 AP, Reaction, 10 MP)
3. **Haunt:** Target must make a Will save or be frightened by terrifying hallucinations for 1 minute. (Cost: 1 AP, 10 MP)

### **Level 3 Abilities (Choose 1):**
1. **Possession:** Send your spirit into a weak enemy (CR 1 or lower). You control their body for 1 minute. (Cost: 1 AP, 20 MP)
2. **Ethereal Chains:** Bind a target's soul. Their movement speed drops to 0, and they take 2d6 psychic damage per turn. (Cost: 1 AP, 15 MP)
3. **Séance:** Summon the spirit of anyone who died in your current location to ask them questions. (Cost: 1 AP, 10 MP)

### **Level 5 Abilities (Choose 1):**
1. **Summon Poltergeist:** Summon a chaotic spirit that throws objects and knocks enemies prone in a 20ft radius. (Cost: 1 AP, 25 MP)
2. **Life Drain:** Deal 3d8 necrotic damage. The target's lost HP is transferred to an ally of your choice. (Cost: 1 AP, 20 MP)
3. **Ghostly Evasion:** For 1 minute, you can move through solid walls and closed doors as if they weren't there. (Cost: 1 AP, 20 MP)

### **Level 7 Abilities (Choose 1):**
1. **Banish Spirit:** Instantly destroy any undead creature or banish an elemental back to its plane (Will save negates). (Cost: 1 AP, 30 MP)
2. **Astral Projection:** Leave your physical body behind entirely, traveling as an invisible, flying ghost anywhere in the world. (Cost: 1 AP, 40 MP)
3. **Wailing Soul:** Release a banshee's scream. Enemies in a 30ft cone take 4d6 psychic damage and are deafened. (Cost: 1 AP, 25 MP)

### **Level 9 Abilities (Choose 1):**
1. **Spirit Ward:** Place a powerful ward on the ground. Undead and fiends physically cannot cross the line. (Cost: 1 AP, 30 MP)
2. **Soul Sever:** Strike an enemy's spirit, dealing 6d10 force damage and reducing their max MP by half. (Cost: 1 AP, 45 MP)
3. **Mass Possession:** Possess up to 3 enemies simultaneously, forcing them all to attack their allies on their next turn. (Cost: 1 AP, 50 MP)

### **Level 11 Abilities (Choose 1):**
1. **Ethereal Storm:** A massive vortex of angry spirits deals 6d8 necrotic and 6d8 cold damage in a 30ft radius. (Cost: 1 AP, 60 MP)
2. **Revive the Fallen:** Call a willing soul back from the afterlife, fully restoring their body and HP. (Cost: 2 AP, 100 MP)
3. **Mind Shatter:** Deal 10d6 psychic damage. If the target survives, their Intelligence drops to 1 permanently (or until cured). (Cost: 1 AP, 65 MP)

### **Level 13 Abilities (Choose 1):**
1. **Army of Ghosts:** Summon 10 spectral warriors who fight for you. They are immune to physical damage. (Cost: 1 AP, 70 MP)
2. **Soul Exchange:** Swap the HP percentages of two creatures you can see (e.g., an enemy at 100% and an ally at 10%). (Cost: 1 AP, 80 MP)
3. **Aura of the Grave:** Enemies within 30ft of you cannot heal, and any healing spells cast on them damage them instead. (Cost: 1 AP, 50 MP)

### **Level 15 Abilities (Choose 1):**
1. **Exorcism:** Violently rip a possessing entity, curse, or magical parasite out of a host, dealing 10d10 damage to the entity. (Cost: 1 AP, 60 MP)
2. **Phase Shift:** You and up to 5 allies shift fully into the Ethereal Plane, escaping danger and traveling undetected. (Cost: 1 AP, 75 MP)
3. **Reaper's Touch:** Melee spell attack. If it hits, the target instantly ages 50 years and takes 8d10 necrotic damage. (Cost: 1 AP, 70 MP)

### **Level 17 Abilities (Choose 1):**
1. **Domain of the Dead:** Pull all enemies within 60ft into the spirit realm. In this realm, they take 4d10 psychic damage every turn. (Cost: 2 AP, 100 MP)
2. **True Possession:** Possess any creature (including Dragons or Gods). If they fail a Will save, you own their body permanently. (Cost: 2 AP, 120 MP)
3. **Shatter Soul:** Deal 20d10 force damage. If the target is killed, their soul is destroyed forever. (Cost: 1 AP, 100 MP)

### **Level 19 Abilities (Choose 1):**
1. **Gate of Souls:** Open a rift to the afterlife. Thousands of spirits pour out, dealing 15d12 damage to an entire army. (Cost: 2 AP, 150 MP)
2. **Ascended Spirit:** You transcend mortality. You no longer have a physical body, rendering you permanently immune to all non-magical damage and physical conditions. (Passive)
3. **Resurrect the Ages:** You can bring back up to 100 dead creatures simultaneously, fully healed, provided you have their names. (Cost: Ritual, 200 MP)

### **Level 2 Skills (Choose 1):**
1. **Ghost Sight:** You can see perfectly in magical darkness and automatically see invisible creatures. (Passive)
2. **Whispers of the Dead:** You gain +5 to Insight and Perception checks, guided by spirits. (Passive)
3. **Float:** You hover 1 foot off the ground, leaving no tracks and ignoring difficult terrain or ground traps. (Passive)

### **Level 4 Skills (Choose 1):**
1. **Soul Bond:** You can telepathically communicate with your allies at any distance, even across planes. (Passive)
2. **Chilling Touch:** Your unarmed strikes deal cold damage instead of bludgeoning, and reduce the target's speed. (Passive)
3. **Ectoplasm Shield:** Gain +20 Max HP that regenerates every long rest. (Passive)

### **Level 6 Skills (Choose 1):**
1. **Sixth Sense:** You cannot be surprised, and you have advantage on initiative rolls. (Passive)
2. **Incorporeal Step:** You can move through other creatures and objects as if they were difficult terrain. (Passive)
3. **Speak with Animals/Plants:** Spirits residing in nature allow you to communicate with all living flora and fauna. (Passive)

### **Level 8 Skills (Choose 1):**
1. **Mind Fortress:** You have advantage on all saving throws against enchantment and illusion spells. (Passive)
2. **Spiritual Weaponry:** Your weapons count as magical and deal force damage instead of their normal type. (Passive)
3. **Aura of Calm:** Allies within 15ft cannot be frightened or charmed. (Passive)

### **Level 10 Skills (Choose 1):**
1. **Master of the Veil:** You can cast *Invisibility* on yourself at will, without expending MP. (Passive)
2. **Death Defier:** If you drop to 0 HP, you instead drop to 1 HP and become ethereal for 1 turn. (Once/day)
3. **Memory Leech:** By touching someone, you can view their most prominent memories from the past 24 hours. (Once/day)

### **Level 12 Skills (Choose 1):**
1. **Ethereal Sight:** You can see into the Ethereal Plane up to 60ft at all times. (Passive)
2. **Soul Drain:** When you kill an enemy, you regain MP equal to their CR or Level. (Passive)
3. **Ghostly Voice:** You can perfectly mimic the voice of any person who has died. (Passive)

### **Level 14 Skills (Choose 1):**
1. **Poltergeist's Vigor:** You can cast *Telekinesis* at will, moving objects up to 1,000 lbs with your mind. (Passive)
2. **Unyielding Spirit:** You gain proficiency in ALL saving throws. (Passive)
3. **Aura of Frost:** The temperature drops around you. Water freezes instantly, and enemies within 10ft take 1d6 cold damage per turn. (Passive)

### **Level 16 Skills (Choose 1):**
1. **Spectral Armor:** You gain resistance to all physical damage types (slashing, piercing, bludgeoning). (Passive)
2. **Spirit Walk:** You can teleport up to 120ft as a bonus action, leaving a terrifying ghostly image behind. (Once/day)
3. **Soul Link:** You can link your life force with an ally. While linked, you both share damage and healing equally. (Passive)

### **Level 18 Skills (Choose 1):**
1. **Immortal Medium:** You stop aging and cannot be aged magically. You are immune to disease and poison. (Passive)
2. **Banishing Strike:** On a critical hit, the target is banished to the spirit realm for 1 minute automatically. (Passive)
3. **Echoes of the Past:** You can touch an object and see everything that has happened around it for the past 1,000 years. (Once/day)

### **Level 20 Skills (Choose 1):**
1. **Avatar of the Afterlife:** You become the judge of souls. You can cast *Wish* once per week to alter reality via the sheer force of collective souls. (Once/week)
2. **Absolute Phasing:** You can exist in two places at once, perfectly duplicating yourself and your actions. (Once/day for 1 minute)
3. **Soul Annihilation:** Your attacks permanently destroy the souls of those you kill, preventing them from ever being resurrected by any means. (Passive)
