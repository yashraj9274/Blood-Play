_"Hunters who fight in perfect synchronization with a fierce animal companion."_
**Mana Dependency** : 2/5
## Class Focus (Single-Class Only)
_Granted at Character Creation. Lost if you multiclass. Choose ONE:_
1. **Soul Link:** Any damage you take can be split evenly with your companion, and any healing either of you receives heals both of you.
2. **Alpha Predator:** If you and your beast attack the same target in the same round, you both deal +1d8 extra damage.
3. **Feral Instinct:** Your beast can take its entire turn (move, attack) independently without costing you any AP to command it.

### **Class Perk (Level 1): Animal Companion**
- You gain a loyal animal companion (Wolf, Bear, Hawk, Panther, etc.).
- Its base HP is equal to your Max HP. Its base ARN is 10 + your RES Mod.
- As an action (1 AP), you can command it to attack, move, or defend.

### **Bonus Perk (Level 10 - Single-Class Only): Beast Lord**
- Your companion grows one size category (e.g., Medium to Large), doubling its base damage dice.
- If your companion dies, you can resurrect it with a 1-hour ritual without spending MP.

--- 

### **Level 1 Abilities (Choose 1):**
1. **Command: Strike:** Command your beast to attack. It uses its natural weapon `(SM+P)d6`. (Cost: 1 AP)
2. **Command: Defend:** Command your beast to protect an ally. The beast intercepts the next attack against them. (Cost: 1 AP)
3. **Hunter's Mark:** Mark a target. You and your beast deal +1d6 damage against it for 1 minute. (Cost: 1 AP)

### **Level 3 Abilities (Choose 1):**
1. **Pounce:** Command your beast to leap 20ft and attack. Target must make a STR save or fall prone. (Cost: 1 AP)
2. **Pack Tactics:** If you and your beast are adjacent to a target, both of your attacks have advantage. (Passive)
3. **Beast's Howl:** Your beast lets out a roar, granting you both temporary AHP equal to your Class Level x 2. (Cost: 1 AP)

### **Level 5 Abilities (Choose 1):**
1. **Coordinated Attack:** When you take the Attack action, your beast can make a free attack at the same time. (Cost: 1 AP)
2. **Harrying Strike:** Your beast attacks a target's legs. The target's speed is halved for 1 turn. (Cost: 1 AP)
3. **Beast's Agility:** Your companion can Disengage or Dash as a free action. (Passive)

### **Level 7 Abilities (Choose 1):**
1. **Furious Swipe:** Your beast attacks all enemies within 5ft of it. (Cost: 1 AP)
2. **Sic 'Em:** Command your beast to grapple a target. The target is restrained until it breaks the grapple. (Cost: 1 AP)
3. **Nature's Ward:** You and your beast gain resistance to all elemental damage (Fire, Cold, Lightning). (Passive)

### **Level 9 Abilities (Choose 1):**
1. **Bestial Fury:** Your beast can make two attacks when commanded to Strike. (Passive)
2. **Takedown:** If your beast attacks a prone target, it deals double damage. (Passive)
3. **Primal Bond:** You can cast spells originating from your beast's location. (Passive)

### **Level 11 Abilities (Choose 1):**
1. **Rampage:** When your beast reduces a creature to 0 HP, it can immediately move and attack another target for free. (Passive)
2. **Stampede:** You and your beast charge in a straight line. Enemies in the path take 4d8 damage. (Cost: 1 AP)
3. **Protector's Sacrifice:** If an attack would kill you, your beast instantly swaps places with you and takes the blow. (Reaction, Passive)

### **Level 13 Abilities (Choose 1):**
1. **Mythic Beast:** Your companion's attacks count as magical for the purpose of overcoming resistances. (Passive)
2. **Savage Rend:** Your beast tears into armor, reducing the target's ARN by 5 on a hit. (Cost: 1 AP)
3. **Apex Roar:** Enemies within 30ft of the beast must make a Will save or be paralyzed with fear for 1 turn. (Cost: 1 AP)

### **Level 15 Abilities (Choose 1):**
1. **Shared Spell:** Any spell you cast that targets yourself also targets your beast companion. (Passive)
2. **Maul:** Your beast pins a target to the ground and bites their neck, dealing 6d10 damage. (Cost: 1 AP)
3. **Evasion:** When subjected to an AoE effect, your beast takes no damage on a successful save, and half on a fail. (Passive)

### **Level 17 Abilities (Choose 1):**
1. **Dire Form:** Your beast permanently transforms into a Dire version of itself. Max HP +100, attacks deal +2d8. (Passive)
2. **Perfect Synergy:** You can mount your beast (if it is large enough). While mounted, your combined speed is 60ft and attacks have advantage. (Passive)
3. **Twin Fangs:** Whenever you critically hit, your beast automatically critically hits its next attack. (Passive)

### **Level 19 Abilities (Choose 1):**
1. **The Wild Hunt:** Summon 5 spectral copies of your beast that all attack a single target simultaneously for 20d6 damage. (Cost: 2 AP)
2. **Undying Loyalty:** If your beast dies, it instantly revives at full HP at the start of your next turn. (Once/day)
3. **Chimera Morph:** Your beast sprouts dragon wings and a scorpion tail. It gains a 60ft fly speed and a poisonous tail attack. (Passive)

### **Level 2 Skills (Choose 1):**
1. **Animal Whisperer:** You can charm and command wild animals. (Passive)
2. **Keen Senses:** You and your beast have advantage on Perception checks involving hearing or smell. (Passive)
3. **Wilderness Guide:** You and your party cannot get lost in natural terrain. (Passive)

### **Level 4 Skills (Choose 1):**
1. **Beast's Vigor:** Your beast's Max HP increases by +30. (Passive)
2. **Feral Stealth:** You and your beast gain +5 to Stealth checks when moving together. (Passive)
3. **Tracking Bond:** You always know the exact location and direction of your beast, no matter how far away. (Passive)

### **Level 6 Skills (Choose 1):**
1. **Natural Medicine:** Heal your beast for 4d6 HP as a free action. (Once/day)
2. **Elemental Claws:** Your beast's attacks deal an extra +1d6 of an element you choose (Fire, Cold, Lightning). (Passive)
3. **Intimidating Presence:** Your beast grants you a +5 bonus to Intimidation checks. (Passive)

### **Level 8 Skills (Choose 1):**
1. **Iron Hide:** Your beast's ARN increases by +5. (Passive)
2. **Beastly Reflexes:** Your beast has advantage on all Reflex saves. (Passive)
3. **Bond of the Wild:** You can see through your beast's eyes and hear through its ears up to 1 mile away. (Passive)

### **Level 10 Skills (Choose 1):**
1. **Resuscitate:** Revive your beast instantly in combat with half HP. (Once/day)
2. **Pack Leader:** All summoned creatures and pets controlled by allies gain +2 to hit. (Passive Aura)
3. **Primal Leap:** Your beast can jump 50ft across gaps or up cliffs while carrying you. (Passive)

### **Level 12 Skills (Choose 1):**
1. **Unbreakable Bond:** You and your beast cannot be charmed or mind-controlled against each other. (Passive)
2. **Feral Speed:** Your beast's movement speed increases by +20ft. (Passive)
3. **Beast's Intuition:** Your beast can sense invisible creatures and illusions within 30ft. (Passive)

### **Level 14 Skills (Choose 1):**
1. **Savage Endurance:** Your beast gains resistance to all physical damage. (Passive)
2. **Roar of Revival:** Your beast's roar heals you and itself for 3d10 HP. (Once/day)
3. **Predator's Cloak:** You and your beast can turn completely invisible in natural terrain. (Once/day for 1 hour)

### **Level 16 Skills (Choose 1):**
1. **Titanic Strength:** Your beast's Strength Score becomes 24. (Passive)
2. **Relentless Tracker:** Once you mark a target, you always know its location, even if it teleports to another plane. (Passive)
3. **Beast's Aegis:** Your beast projects a magical barrier, granting it 500 AHP. (Once/day)

### **Level 18 Skills (Choose 1):**
1. **Soul Merge:** You merge physically with your beast, gaining its HP on top of yours and dealing double damage for 1 minute. (Once/day)
2. **Immortal Companion:** Your beast cannot be killed by any instant-death effects or magic. (Passive)
3. **Lord of the Wilds:** Every wild animal in a 1-mile radius comes to your aid in battle. (Once/day)

### **Level 20 Skills (Choose 1):**
1. **God-Beast:** Your companion ascends to godhood. Its stats are doubled, and it can speak any language. (Passive)
2. **Perfect Hunt:** You and your beast automatically critically hit on all attacks for 1 round. (Once/day)
3. **One Heart:** If either you or your beast drops to 0 HP, the other can sacrifice half their current HP to instantly restore the fallen one to full health. (Once/day)
