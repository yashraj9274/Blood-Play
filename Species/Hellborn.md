## 🧬 Traits
*Copy these directly into the "Race Traits" section of your Character Sheet.*

### Race Traits
* **Hellish Resistance:** Fire damage reduced by (3d6) + PC level. Additionally, you have advantage on saving throws against fire-based effects.
* **Infernal Legacy:** can use flamethrower once per day (DMG=(5d10)+PC level)
* **Darkvision:** You can see in dim light within 60 feet as if it were bright light, and in darkness as if it were dim light. In darkness, you see in shades of red.
